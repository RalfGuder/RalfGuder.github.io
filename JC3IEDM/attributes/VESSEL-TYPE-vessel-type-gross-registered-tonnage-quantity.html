Attribute
Name: vessel-type-gross-registered-tonnage-quantity
Definition: The numeric value that represents a ship's internal cubic capacity or freight-carrying capacity. The unit of measure is Gross Registered Tonnage (GRT). A unit of Gross Registered Tonnage is equal to 2.83 cubic metres.
Domain: quantity
Other Attribute Properties
This attribute is optional in the entity VESSEL-TYPE
