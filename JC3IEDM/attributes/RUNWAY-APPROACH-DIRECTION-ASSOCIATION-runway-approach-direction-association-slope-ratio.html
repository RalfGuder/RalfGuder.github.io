Attribute
Name: runway-approach-direction-association-slope-ratio
Definition: The numeric quotient value that represents the incline of the runway seen from the direction of the APPROACH-DIRECTION as proportion of vertical change with respect to the length of the runway. The value must be in the range from -.09 to +.09. Note: A negative value indicates a downward slope.
Domain: ratio
Other Attribute Properties
This attribute is optional in the entity RUNWAY-APPROACH-DIRECTION-ASSOCIATION
