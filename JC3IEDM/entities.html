VEHICLE-TYPE An EQUIPMENT-TYPE that is designed to operate on land routes (other than rail) with a primary role of transporting personnel, equipment or supplies.
VERTICAL-DISTANCE A specification of the altitude or height of a point or a level as measured with respect to a specified reference datum in the direction normal to the plane that is tangent to the WGS84 ellipsoid of revolution.
VESSEL-TYPE An EQUIPMENT-TYPE that is designed to operate on or under the water surface.
