Entity
Name: ACTION-TASK-STATUS
Definition: The perceived appraisal of the planning and execution progress of a particular ACTION-TASK as determined by the reporting organisation.

Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
action-task-id (PK) (FK) MA The action-id of a specific ACTION-TASK (a role name for action-id).
action-task-status-index (PK) MA The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific ACTION-TASK-STATUS for a specific ACTION-TASK and to distinguish it from all other ACTION-TASK-STATUSs for that ACTION-TASK.

action-task-status-planning-indicator-code OP The specific value that denotes at the reporting time whether an ACTION-TASK is completed in the planning process.
action-task-status-progress-code OP The specific value that represents the perceived appraisal of the progress of a specific ACTION-TASK.
