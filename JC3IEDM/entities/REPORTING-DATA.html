Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
reporting-data-id (PK) MA The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific REPORTING-DATA and to distinguish it from all other REPORTING-DATAs.
reference-id (FK) OP The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific REFERENCE and to distinguish it from all other REFERENCEs.
reporting-data-accuracy-code OP The specific value that represents, for intelligence purpose, the general appraisal of the subject matter in graded terms to indicate the extent or degree to which it has been judged to be free from mistake or error or to conform to truth or some recognised standard value.
reporting-data-category-code OP The specific value that represents, for usual operational purposes, the nature of a specific REPORTING-DATA.
reporting-data-counting-indicator-code OP The specific value that denotes whether the data referred to by a specific REPORTING-DATA is based on a count of objects.
reporting-data-credibility-code OP The specific value that represents, for normal operational use, the degree of trustworthiness of the data referenced by a specific REPORTING-DATA.
reporting-data-real-data-exercise-use-only-code OP The specific value that determines whether a specific REPORTING-DATA refers to real data in an exercise scenario.
reporting-data-reliability-code OP The specific value that represents, for intelligence purpose, the general appraisal of the source in graded terms to indicate the extent to which it has been proven it can be counted on or trusted in to do as expected.
reporting-data-reporting-datetime OP The character string representing a point in time that designates when the data referenced by the REPORTING-DATA is provided.
reporting-data-reporting-organisation-id (FK) OP The identifier of the ORGANISATION that is responsible for providing the data that is referenced by the specific REPORTING-DATA (a role name for object-item-id).
reporting-data-source-type-code OP The specific value that identifies the source type from which intelligence information is obtained and which is referred to by a specific REPORTING-DATA.
reporting-data-timing-category-code OP The specific value that represents the absolute, uncertain or relative timing for a specific REPORTING-DATA. It serves as a discriminator that partitions REPORTING-DATA into subtypes.
