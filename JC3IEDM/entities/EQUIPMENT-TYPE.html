Entity
Name: EQUIPMENT-TYPE
Definition: A MATERIEL-TYPE that is not intended for consumption.
Supertype: MATERIEL-TYPE
Subtypes: AIRCRAFT-TYPE, CBRN-EQUIPMENT-TYPE, ELECTRONIC-EQUIPMENT-TYPE, ENGINEERING-EQUIPMENT-TYPE, MARITIME-EQUIPMENT-TYPE, MISCELLANEOUS-EQUIPMENT-TYPE, RAILCAR-TYPE, VEHICLE-TYPE, VESSEL-TYPE, WEAPON-TYPE

Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
equipment-type-id (PK) (FK) MA The materiel-type-id of a specific EQUIPMENT-TYPE (a role name for object-type-id).
equipment-type-category-code OP The specific value that represents the class of EQUIPMENT-TYPE. It serves as a discriminator that partitions EQUIPMENT-TYPE into subtypes.
equipment-type-fuel-capacity-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the usable fuel capacity of an EQUIPMENT-TYPE. The unit of measure is litre.
equipment-type-loaded-weight-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the weight of a specific EQUIPMENT-TYPE including the normal maximum payload, crew, and personal/organisation equipment as well as the basic issue items. The unit of measure is kilogram.
equipment-type-maximum-height-dimension OP The one-dimensional linear distance representing the maximum distance measured perpendicular to the base plane of the specific EQUIPMENT-TYPE.
equipment-type-maximum-length-dimension OP The one-dimensional linear distance representing the maximum distance measured from end to end and parallel to the central axis of a specific EQUIPMENT-TYPE.
equipment-type-maximum-width-dimension OP The one-dimensional linear distance representing the maximum distance measured from side to side and perpendicular to the central axis of a specific EQUIPMENT-TYPE.
equipment-type-unloaded-weight-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the weight of a specific EQUIPMENT-TYPE including on-equipment materiel that is an integral part of the equipment when issued. The unit of measure is kilogram.
