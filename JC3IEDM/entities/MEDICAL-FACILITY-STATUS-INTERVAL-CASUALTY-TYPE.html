Entity
Name: MEDICAL-FACILITY-STATUS-INTERVAL-CASUALTY-TYPE
Definition: A MEDICAL-FACILITY-STATUS that specifies the count of casualty arrivals and admissions in each of specified groups according to specified medical classification during the period defined by the effective beginning and ending datetimes stipulated through REPORTING-DATA.
