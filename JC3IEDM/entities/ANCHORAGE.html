Entity
Name: ANCHORAGE
Definition: A FACILITY that is a place where vessels anchor.
Supertype: FACILITY
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
anchorage-id (PK) (FK) MA The facility-id of a specific ANCHORAGE (a role name for object-item-id).
anchorage-bottom-type-code OP The specific value that represents the description of the ground under the water of a lake, river, ocean, or other body of water at a specific ANCHORAGE.
anchorage-day-limit-net-explosive-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the maximum net explosive quantity that can be handled at the ANCHORAGE during the day. The unit of measure is kilogram.
anchorage-draught-high-tide-dimension OP The one-dimensional linear distance representing the maximum draught of vessel at high tide that the specific ANCHORAGE can accommodate.
anchorage-draught-low-tide-dimension OP The one-dimensional linear distance representing the maximum draught of vessel at low tide that the specific ANCHORAGE can accommodate.
anchorage-moorings-type-code OP The specific value that represents the class of mooring available at the specific ANCHORAGE.
anchorage-night-limit-net-explosive-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the maximum net explosive quantity that can be handled at the ANCHORAGE during the night. The unit of measure is kilogram.
anchorage-prevailing-wind-direction-code OP The specific value that represents the direction of the wind that most frequently occurs for the specific ANCHORAGE.
anchorage-vessel-tonnage-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the maximum tonnage of a vessel that can be accommodated at a specific ANCHORAGE. The unit of measure is metric ton.
