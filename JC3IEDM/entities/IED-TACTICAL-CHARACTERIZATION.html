Entity
Name: IED-TACTICAL-CHARACTERIZATION
Definition: An ACTION-EVENT-DETAIL that describes how an IED incident was conducted or intended to be conducted.
Supertype: ACTION-EVENT-DETAIL
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
action-event-detail-index (PK) (FK) MA The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific ACTION-EVENT-DETAIL for a specific ACTION-EVENT and to distinguish it from all other ACTION-EVENT-DETAILs for that ACTION-EVENT.
action-event-id (PK) (FK) MA The action-id of a specific ACTION-EVENT (a role name for action-id).
ied-tactical-characterization-emplacement-code OP The specific value that describes where the device is positioned.
ied-tactical-characterization-employment-method-code OP The specific value that describes how the device is delivered or attached to the target.
ied-tactical-characterization-suicide-code OP The specific value that indicates whether an IED was initiated by an operator at a time of his/her choosing in which the operator intentionally kills himself/herself as part of the attack.
ied-tactical-characterization-use-sequence-code OP The specific value that identifies enemy use of an IED as a primary, secondary, or tertiary form of attack, independent of intended outcome.
ied-tactical-characterization-vehicle-placement-code OP The specific value that indicates whether an IED is attached to the underside of a vehicle.
