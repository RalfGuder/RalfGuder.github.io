Name: ENGINEERING-CAPABILITY
Definition: A CAPABILITY, required for planning, of those ORGANISATIONs and PERSONs or ORGANISATION-TYPEs and PERSON-TYPEs that are deemed as having the ability to perform construction or destruction activities.
