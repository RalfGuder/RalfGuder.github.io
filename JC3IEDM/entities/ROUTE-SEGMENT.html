Entity
Name: ROUTE-SEGMENT
Definition: A portion of a route usually without an intermediate stop, as defined by two consecutive significant points.
Supertype: CONTROL-FEATURE
Subtypes: AIR-ROUTE-SEGMENT
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
route-segment-id (PK) (FK) MA The control-feature-id of a specific ROUTE-SEGMENT (a role name for object-item-id).
route-segment-category-code OP The specific value that represents the class of ROUTE-SEGMENT. It serves as a discriminator that partitions ROUTE-SEGMENT into subtypes.
route-segment-mobility-code OP The specific value that indicates the suitability of a specific ROUTE-SEGMENT for movement.
route-segment-mode-of-transportation-code OP The specific value that indicates the mode of transportation of a specific ROUTE-SEGMENT.
