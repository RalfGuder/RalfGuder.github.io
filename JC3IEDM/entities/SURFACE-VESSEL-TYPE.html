Entity
Name: SURFACE-VESSEL-TYPE
Definition: A vessel principally designed to operate on the water surface.
Supertype: VESSEL-TYPE
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
surface-vessel-type-id (PK) (FK) MA The vessel-type-id of a specific SURFACE-VESSEL-TYPE (a role name for object-type-id).
surface-vessel-type-category-code OP The specific value that represents the class of surface vessel.
surface-vessel-type-displacement-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the maximum volume of water moved by the vessel when it is fully loaded. The unit of measure is cubic metre.
surface-vessel-type-maximum-deck-load-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the Ship's maximum allowable deck load. The unit of measure is kilogram.
