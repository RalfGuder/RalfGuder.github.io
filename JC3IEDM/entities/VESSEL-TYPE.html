Entity
Name: VESSEL-TYPE
Definition: An EQUIPMENT-TYPE that is designed to operate on or under the water surface.
Supertype: EQUIPMENT-TYPE
Subtypes: SUBSURFACE-VESSEL-TYPE, SURFACE-VESSEL-TYPE
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
vessel-type-id (PK) (FK) MA The equipment-type-id of a specific VESSEL-TYPE (a role name for object-type-id).
vessel-type-acoustic-merit-index-quantity OP The numeric value that indicates the total acoustic level.
vessel-type-category-code OP The specific value that represents the class of VESSEL-TYPE. It serves as a discriminator that partitions VESSEL-TYPE into subtypes.
vessel-type-dead-weight-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the carrying capacity of a ship. Dead weight is the difference between the Full displacement (Gross weight) and the Light displacement (Net weight). The unit of measure is metric ton.
vessel-type-draught-dimension OP The numeric value of the distance from the Deep Water Line (DWL) to the lowest permanent projection on the hull of a vessel type including sonar domes, propellers, rudders, or other projections.
vessel-type-gross-registered-tonnage-quantity OP The numeric value that represents a ship's internal cubic capacity or freight-carrying capacity. The unit of measure is Gross Registered Tonnage (GRT). A unit of Gross Registered Tonnage is equal to 2.83 cubic metres.
vessel-type-height-above-the-waterline-dimension OP The one-dimensional linear distance representing the distance from the waterline to the topmost point of an unloaded vessel.
vessel-type-magnetic-degaussing-code-number-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the peak vertical component of the magnetic field under a ship on the worst heading and at certain depth. The unit of measure is microtesla.
vessel-type-maximum-speed-rate OP The numeric value of the maximum speed that a vessel type can maintain for one hour or less with a clean hull immediately out of dry docking or refit. The speed is measured in knots. The specified value must be greater than or equal to 0 (zero).
vessel-type-operational-displacement-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the weight or volume of water moved by the vessel on the surface of the water. The unit of measure is metric ton.
vessel-type-prismatic-coefficient-ratio OP The numeric quotient value that represents a ratio of the volume displaced by the hull in relation to the volume of a prism or cylinder of cross section equal to the greatest cross-section of the submerged part of the hull and of length equal to the ships length between perpendiculars. The value must be in the range from 0 to 1.
vessel-type-propeller-count OP The integer value representing the number of propellers on the ship.
vessel-type-propulsion-type-code OP The specific value that represents the type of power used to move the vessel.
