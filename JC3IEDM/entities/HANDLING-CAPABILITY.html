Entity
Name: HANDLING-CAPABILITY
Definition: A CAPABILITY, required for planning, of those FACILITYs and MATERIELs, or FACILITY-TYPEs and EQUIPMENT-TYPEs that are deemed as having the ability to move materiels (raw materials, scrap, semi-finished, and finished) to, through, and from productive processes; in warehouses and storage; and in receiving and shipping areas.
Supertype: CAPABILITY
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
handling-capability-id (PK) (FK) MA The capability-id of a specific HANDLING-CAPABILITY (a role name for capability-id).
handling-capability-action-code OP The specific value that represents the type of a specific HANDLING-CAPABILITY.
handling-capability-cargo-category-code OP The specific value that represents the class of cargo subject to the HANDLING-CAPABILITY.
handling-capability-descriptor-code OP The specific value that represents the HANDLING-CAPABILITY that is being quantified.
