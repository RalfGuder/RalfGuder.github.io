Entity
Name: AIRFIELD
Definition: A FACILITY that is an area prepared for the accommodation (including any buildings, installations, or equipment) of landing and take off of aircraft.
Supertype: FACILITY
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
airfield-id (PK) (FK) MA The facility-id of a specific AIRFIELD (a role name for object-item-id).
airfield-air-traffic-control-presence-indicator-code OP The specific value that indicates whether a specific AIRFIELD provides air traffic control.
airfield-hangar-area-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the total hangar area in a specific AIRFIELD. The unit of measure is square metre.
airfield-instrument-landing-system-presence-indicator-code OP The specific value that indicates whether a specific AIRFIELD has an instrument landing system.
airfield-international-civil-aviation-organisation-text OP The character string assigned to represent the description the international civil aviation organization (ICAO) identifier commonly used throughout the world for reference to a known aviation facility.
airfield-visual-navigational-aid-indicator-code OP The specific value indicating whether or not the airport has a visual navigational aid displaying flashes of white or colored light to indicate the location of an airport.
