Entity
Name: SURVEILLANCE-CAPABILITY
Definition: A CAPABILITY, required for planning, of those FACILITYs, MATERIELs, ORGANISATIONs and PERSONs or FACILITY-TYPEs, EQUIPMENT-TYPEs, ORGANISATION-TYPEs and PERSON-TYPEs that are deemed as having the nominal ability to observe aerospace, surface or subsurface areas, places, persons, or things, by visual, aural, electronic, photographic or other means.
Supertype: CAPABILITY
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
surveillance-capability-id (PK) (FK) MA The capability-id of a specific SURVEILLANCE-CAPABILITY (a role name for capability-id).
surveillance-capability-category-code OP The specific value that represents the class of SURVEILLANCE-CAPABILITY.
surveillance-capability-descriptor-code OP The specific value that represents the SURVEILLANCE-CAPABILITY that is being quantified.
