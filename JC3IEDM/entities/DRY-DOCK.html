Entity
Name: DRY-DOCK
Definition: A FACILITY that provides an enclosure for maintenance, building or repairing ships, from which water can be pumped out.
Supertype: FACILITY
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
dry-dock-id (PK) (FK) MA The facility-id of a specific DRY-DOCK (a role name for object-item-id).
dry-dock-boat-lift-capacity-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the maximum tonnage of a boat-lift that can be utilised to remove a vessel from a specific DRY-DOCK. The unit of measure is metric ton.
dry-dock-depth-dimension OP The one-dimensional linear distance representing the depth of water available at the DRY-DOCK when the dock is full of water.
dry-dock-location-text OP The character string assigned to represent a statement of the details that relate to the specific DRY-DOCK.
dry-dock-marine-railway-size-code OP The specific value that represents the bearing capacity of the underwater railway in the DRY-DOCK.
