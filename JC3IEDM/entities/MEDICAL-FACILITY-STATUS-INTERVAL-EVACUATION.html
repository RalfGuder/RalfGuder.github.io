Entity
Name: MEDICAL-FACILITY-STATUS-INTERVAL-EVACUATION
Definition: A MEDICAL-FACILITY-STATUS that specifies the count of casualties evacuated in each of specified groups according to the intended destination during the period defined by the effective beginning and ending datetimes stipulated through REPORTING-DATA.
