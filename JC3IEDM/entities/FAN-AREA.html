Entity
Name: FAN-AREA
Definition: A SURFACE that is in the form of a truncated ring sector, lying between and being bounded by the rays emanating from the centre-point of the ring and having a specified central angle.
Supertype: SURFACE
Cardinality relationships:

Name Opt Definition
fan-area-id (PK) (FK) MA The surface-id of a specific FAN-AREA (a role name for location-id).
fan-area-maximum-range-dimension OP The one-dimensional linear distance representing distance from the vertex to the outer ring of the ring sector used to specify the FAN-AREA.
fan-area-minimum-range-dimension OP The one-dimensional linear distance representing the distance from the vertex to the inner ring of the ring sector used to specify the FAN-AREA.
fan-area-orientation-angle OP The rotational measurement clockwise between the line of true north and the left side of the sector for a specific FAN-AREA.
fan-area-sector-size-angle OP The rotational measurement clockwise between the left and right sides of the sector for a specific FAN-AREA.
fan-area-vertex-point-id (FK) OP The point-id of a specific POINT that defines the vertex for the specific FAN-AREA (a role name for location-id).
