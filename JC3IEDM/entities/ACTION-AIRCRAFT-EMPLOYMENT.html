Entity
Name: ACTION-AIRCRAFT-EMPLOYMENT
Definition: The procedure that guides the use of an ACTION-RESOURCE that is capable of atmospheric flight.
Supertype: ACTION-RESOURCE-EMPLOYMENT
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
action-id (PK) (FK) MA The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific ACTION and to distinguish it from all other ACTIONs.
action-resource-employment-index (PK) (FK) MA The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific ACTION-RESOURCE-EMPLOYMENT for a specific ACTION-RESOURCE and to distinguish it from all other ACTION-RESOURCE-EMPLOYMENTs for that ACTION-RESOURCE.
action-resource-index (PK) (FK) MA The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific ACTION-RESOURCE for a specific ACTION and to distinguish it from all other ACTION-RESOURCEs for that ACTION.
action-aircraft-employment-approach-offset-code OP The specific value that represents the side of the initial point-to-target line that the attack aircraft is cleared to manoeuvre in during the target run.
action-aircraft-employment-crew-composition-code OP The specific value that represents the composition of the crew.
action-aircraft-employment-deplanement-method-code OP The specific value that represents the standard method of deplanement of a specific ACTION-RESOURCE in support of a specific ACTION.
action-aircraft-employment-egress-direction-angle OP The rotational measurement clockwise from the line of true north to the direction to be used by an aircraft when departing an ACTION-OBJECTIVE.
action-aircraft-employment-general-role-code OP The specific value that represents the general operational role of an aircraft.
action-aircraft-employment-inflight-report-requirement-indicator-code OP The specific value that represents whether there is a requirement for the flight leader to provide a report of mission accomplishments.
action-aircraft-employment-ingress-direction-angle OP The rotational measurement clockwise from the line of true North to the direction to be used by an aircraft when approaching an ACTION-OBJECTIVE. Includes the concept of terminal attack direction.
