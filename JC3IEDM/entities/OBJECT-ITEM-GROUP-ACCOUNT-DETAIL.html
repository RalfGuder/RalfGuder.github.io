Entity
Name: OBJECT-ITEM-GROUP-ACCOUNT-DETAIL
Definition: The total count and condition of a specific group included in a specific OBJECT-ITEM-GROUP-ACCOUNT. The group is defined as a specific PERSON-TYPE that may also be categorised by a specific GROUP-CHARACTERISTIC.
