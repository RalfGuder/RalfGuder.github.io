Entity
Name: UXO-STATUS
Definition: A MATERIEL-STATUS that is a record of the condition of an explosive ordnance that has been primed, fused, armed, or otherwise prepared for action, and which has been fired, dropped, launched, placed in such a manner, as to constitute a hazard to operation, and remains unexploded either by malfunction or for any other cause.
Supertype: MATERIEL-STATUS
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
object-item-status-index (PK) (FK) MA The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific OBJECT-ITEM-STATUS for a specific OBJECT-ITEM and to distinguish it from all other OBJECT-ITEM-STATUSs for that OBJECT-ITEM.
uxo-status-id (PK) (FK) MA The materiel-id of the MATERIEL that is the subject of a specific UXO-STATUS (a role name for object-item-id).
uxo-status-exposure-code OP The specific value that represents the visual status of a specific Unexploded Explosive Ordnance.
uxo-status-qualifier-code OP The specific value that represents the qualification status of a specific Unexploded Explosive Ordnance.
