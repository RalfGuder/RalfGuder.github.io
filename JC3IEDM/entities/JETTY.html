Entity
Name: JETTY
Definition: A FACILITY that is a platform that may be fixed or floating extending from a shore, normally attached to a wharf or the shore, and which allows access to a vessel lying alongside, used to secure, protect and provide landing and docking for vessels.
Supertype: FACILITY
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
jetty-id (PK) (FK) MA The facility-id of a specific JETTY (a role name for object-item-id).
jetty-day-limit-net-explosive-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the maximum net explosive quantity that can be handled at the JETTY during the day. The unit of measure is kilogram.
jetty-maximum-deadweight-tonnage-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the maximum deadweight tonnage for a vessel at the specific JETTY. The unit of measure is metric ton.
jetty-maximum-draught-dimension OP The one-dimensional linear distance representing the maximum draught of vessel that the specific JETTY can accommodate.
jetty-night-limit-net-explosive-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the maximum net explosive quantity that can be handled at the JETTY during the night. The unit of measure is kilogram.
jetty-rail-capacity-count OP The integer value that represents the maximum number of goods/freight cars that the JETTY can accommodate at the same time.
jetty-rail-served-indicator-code OP The specific value that represents whether the JETTY has railway facilities.
jetty-vessel-maximum-beam-dimension OP The one-dimensional linear distance representing the maximum beam or width of vessel that the specific JETTY can accommodate.
