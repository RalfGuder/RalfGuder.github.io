Entity
Name: MINE-STATUS
Definition: A MATERIEL-STATUS that is a record of condition of a specific mine.
Supertype: MATERIEL-STATUS
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
mine-status-id (PK) (FK) MA The materiel-id of the MATERIEL that is the subject of a specific MINE-STATUS (a role name for object-item-id).
object-item-status-index (PK) (FK) MA The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific OBJECT-ITEM-STATUS for a specific OBJECT-ITEM and to distinguish it from all other OBJECT-ITEM-STATUSs for that OBJECT-ITEM.
mine-status-air-drop-effect-code OP The specific value that represent the behaviour of air-delivered mine after weapon release.
mine-status-code OP The specific value that represents the status of a mine.
mine-status-maritime-mine-qualifier-code OP The specific value that represents the qualification status of a specific maritime mine.
mine-status-mine-buried-ratio OP The numeric quotient value that represents the percentage of the maritime mine that is buried. The value must be in the range from 0 to 1.
