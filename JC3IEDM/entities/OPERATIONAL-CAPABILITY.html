Entity
Name: OPERATIONAL-CAPABILITY
Definition: A CAPABILITY, required for planning, of those objects and types of objects that are deemed as having the ability, the training and the equipment to perform an operation.
Supertype: CAPABILITY
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
operational-capability-id (PK) (FK) MA The capability-id of a specific OPERATIONAL-CAPABILITY (a role name for capability-id).
operational-capability-category-code OP The specific value that identifies a particular OPERATIONAL-CAPABILITY.
operational-capability-level-code OP The specific value that represents the force level at which the specific OPERATIONAL-CAPABILITY is intended to be performed.
operational-capability-qualifier-code OP The specific value that represents the degree to which the specific OPERATIONAL-CAPABILITY can be fulfilled.
