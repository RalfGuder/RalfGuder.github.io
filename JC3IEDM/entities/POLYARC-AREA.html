Entity
Name: POLYARC-AREA
Definition: A SURFACE that consists of a circular arc and a polygonal segment defined by a specific LINE whose beginning coincides with the initial point of the arc and whose end coincides with the last point of the arc.
Supertype: SURFACE

Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
polyarc-area-id (PK) (FK) MA The surface-id of a specific POLYARC-AREA (a role name for location-id).
polyarc-area-arc-radius-dimension OP The one-dimensional linear distance representing the distance from the vertex to the ring sector used to define part of a specific POLYARC-AREA.

polyarc-area-end-bearing-angle OP The rotational measurement clockwise from true North to the right side of a horizontal conical section used in defining a specific POLYARC-AREA.
