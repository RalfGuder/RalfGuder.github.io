Entity
Name: PERSON-STATUS
Definition: An OBJECT-ITEM-STATUS that is a record of condition of a specific PERSON.
Supertype: OBJECT-ITEM-STATUS
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
object-item-status-index (PK) (FK) MA The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific OBJECT-ITEM-STATUS for a specific OBJECT-ITEM and to distinguish it from all other OBJECT-ITEM-STATUSs for that OBJECT-ITEM.
person-status-id (PK) (FK) MA The person-id of the PERSON that is the subject of a specific PERSON-STATUS (a role name for object-item-id).
person-status-duty-status-code OP The specific value that represents the availability of a specific PERSON for duty at a military or civilian post of employment.
person-status-physical-status-code OP The specific value that represents the general physical status of a specific PERSON.
person-status-physical-status-qualifier-code OP The specific value that qualifies the health conditions of a specific PERSON at a specific point in time.
person-status-radiation-dose-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the total radiation dose to which a person has been exposed. The unit of measure is centiGray (cGy).
person-status-reserve-indicator-code OP The specific value that represents whether a specific PERSON has been placed in reserve.
