Entity
Name: FACILITY
Definition: An OBJECT-ITEM that is built, installed or established to serve some particular purpose and is identified by the service it provides rather than by its content.
Supertype: OBJECT-ITEM
Subtypes: AIRFIELD, ANCHORAGE, APRON, BASIN, BERTH, BRIDGE, DRY-DOCK, HARBOUR, JETTY, MILITARY-OBSTACLE, NETWORK, QUAY, RAILWAY, ROAD, RUNWAY, SLIPWAY
