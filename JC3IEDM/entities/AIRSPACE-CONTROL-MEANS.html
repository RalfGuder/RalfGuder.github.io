Entity
Name: AIRSPACE-CONTROL-MEANS
Definition: A CONTROL-FEATURE that reserves airspace for specific airspace users, restricts the action of airspace users, controls the actions of specific airspace users, and/or requires airspace users to accomplish specific actions.
Supertype: CONTROL-FEATURE
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
airspace-control-means-id (PK) (FK) MA The control-feature-id of a specific AIRSPACE-CONTROL-MEANS (a role name for object-item-id).
airspace-control-means-transit-instruction-text OP The character string assigned to represent the specific transit instructions for a specific airspace.
