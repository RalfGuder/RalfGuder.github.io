Entity
Name: CONTROL-FEATURE-STATUS
Definition: An OBJECT-ITEM-STATUS that is a record of condition of a specific CONTROL-FEATURE.
Supertype: OBJECT-ITEM-STATUS
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
control-feature-status-id (PK) (FK) MA The control-feature-id of the CONTROL-FEATURE that is the subject of a specific CONTROL-FEATURE-STATUS (a role name for object-item-id).
object-item-status-index (PK) (FK) MA The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific OBJECT-ITEM-STATUS for a specific OBJECT-ITEM and to distinguish it from all other OBJECT-ITEM-STATUSs for that OBJECT-ITEM.
control-feature-status-cbrn-threat-level-code OP The specific value that represents the assessed CBRN threat level status of a specific CONTROL-FEATURE that defines a given operational area for friendly forces.
control-feature-status-investigation-status-code OP The specific value that represents the investigation status of the site encompassed by a specific CONTROL-FEATURE.
control-feature-status-security-status-code OP The specific value that represents the protection status of the site encompassed by a specific CONTROL-FEATURE.
control-feature-status-usage-status-code OP The specific value that represents the usage of a specific CONTROL-FEATURE.
