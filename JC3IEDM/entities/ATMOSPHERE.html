Entity
Name: ATMOSPHERE
Definition: A METEOROLOGIC-FEATURE that specifies humidity, pressure, and temperature characteristics of Earth's atmosphere.
Supertype: METEOROLOGIC-FEATURE
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
atmosphere-id (PK) (FK) MA The meteorologic-feature-id of a specific ATMOSPHERE (a role name for object-item-id).
atmosphere-humidity-ratio OP The numeric quotient value that represents the proportion of water present in the air to the maximum amount of water (saturation point) possible at a given temperature and pressure. The value must be in the range from 0 to 1.
atmosphere-inversion-layer-code OP The specific value that represents the height of the inversion layer in the atmosphere. The stability class describes the degree of mixing of released material in the atmosphere.
atmosphere-pressure-quantity OP The numeric value that denotes the ambient air pressure in terms of force per unit area. The unit of measure is kPa (Kilopascals).
atmosphere-pressure-system-category-code OP The specific value that represents the class of a pressure system in a particular ATMOSPHERE.
atmosphere-temperature OP The numeric value that indicates the heat of the ambient air for a specific ATMOSPHERE.
atmosphere-temperature-gradient-code OP The specific value that represents heat change with respect to the ground and 100 m in elevation in a certain area. Acts as an indication of vertical air movement between the ground and higher elevations.
