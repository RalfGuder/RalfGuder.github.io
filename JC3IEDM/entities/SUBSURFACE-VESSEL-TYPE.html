Entity
Name: SUBSURFACE-VESSEL-TYPE
Definition: A vessel principally designed to operate under the water surface.
Supertype: VESSEL-TYPE
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
subsurface-vessel-type-id (PK) (FK) MA The vessel-type-id of a specific SUBSURFACE-VESSEL-TYPE (a role name for object-type-id).
subsurface-vessel-type-category-code OP The specific value that represents the class of subsurface vessel.
subsurface-vessel-type-dived-displacement-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the volume of water that is moved by the subsurface vessel when it is entirely below the surface. The unit of measure is ton.
subsurface-vessel-type-speed-cavitation-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the speed at which the subsurface vessel will form bubbles or a vacuum in the water. The unit of measure is knots.
subsurface-vessel-type-torpedo-loading-gear-indicator-code OP The specific value that indicates whether a subsurface vessel has torpedo loading rails and lifting bands.
