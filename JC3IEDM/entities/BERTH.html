Entity
Name: BERTH
Definition: A FACILITY that is a space or length in the water at a harbour allocated to or reserved for a vessel to dock and moor for loading or unloading.
Supertype: FACILITY
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
berth-id (PK) (FK) MA The facility-id of a specific BERTH (a role name for object-item-id).
berth-day-limit-net-explosive-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the maximum net explosive quantity that can be handled at the BERTH during the day. The unit of measure is kilogram.
berth-deadweight-tonnage-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the maximum deadweight tonnage that can be accommodated for a vessel at the specific BERTH. The unit of measure is metric ton.
berth-depth-dimension OP The one-dimensional linear distance representing the depth of water available at the BERTH at low tide.
berth-location-text OP The character string assigned to represent a statement of the details that relate to the specific BERTH.
berth-major-vessel-class-code OP The specific value that represents the class of vessels to be serviced.
berth-maximum-beam-dimension OP The one-dimensional linear distance representing the width athwartships, including all projections, of the largest vessel a specific BERTH can process.
berth-maximum-capacity-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the maximum tonnage a specific BERTH can process per day. The unit of measure is Gross Registered Tonnage (GRT).
berth-maximum-vessel-count OP The integer value representing the maximum number of vessels a specific BERTH can process at the same time.
berth-night-limit-net-explosive-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the maximum net explosive quantity that can be handled at the BERTH during the night. The unit of measure is kilogram.
berth-rail-availability-indicator-code OP The specific value that represents the availability of railroad transportation capability at a specific BERTH.
berth-roll-on-roll-off-indicator-code OP The specific value that represents whether or not the BERTH has roll on/roll off capabilities.
berth-turnaround-time-duration OP The numeric value that represents a quantity of time in milliseconds representing the average units of time for the process of docking, unloading, reloading and undocking a ship for a specific BERTH.
