Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
action-task-id (PK) (FK) MA The action-id of a specific ACTION-TASK (a role name for action-id).
action-task-activity-code OP The specific value that represents the type of activity prescribed by the ACTION-TASK.
action-task-category-code OP The specific value that represents the class of ACTION-TASK. It serves as a discriminator that partitions ACTION-TASK into subtypes.
action-task-detail-text OP The character string assigned to represent the description of an ACTION-TASK's detail.
action-task-end-qualifier-code OP The specific value that denotes the role of ending date and time with respect to the period of effectiveness of a specific ACTION-TASK.
action-task-entailed-safety-degree-code OP The specific value that represents the degree of safety (or risk) entailed with an ordered operation.
action-task-estimated-duration OP The numeric value that represents a quantity of time in milliseconds for the estimated period of effectiveness of a specific ACTION-TASK.
action-task-maximum-duration OP The numeric value that represents a quantity of time in milliseconds for the maximum permissible period of effectiveness of a specific ACTION-TASK.
action-task-meteorological-impact-code OP The specific value that represents a subjective indication of the effect of weather conditions on a specific operation.
action-task-minimum-duration OP The numeric value that represents a quantity of time in milliseconds for the minimum permissible period of effectiveness of a specific ACTION-TASK.
action-task-operational-level-code OP The specific value that represents the operational level of the specific ACTION-TASK.
action-task-overt-covert-code OP The specific value that represents the property of an ACTION-TASK to be overt or covert.
action-task-planned-end-datetime OP The character string representing a point in time that designates the occurrence of the planned conclusion of the specific ACTION-TASK.
action-task-planned-start-datetime OP The character string representing a point in time that designates the occurrence of the planned beginning of the specific ACTION-TASK.
action-task-priority-code OP The specific value that represents the rank of importance of a specific ACTION-TASK in view of the planning organisation.
action-task-start-qualifier-code OP The specific value that denotes the role of starting date and time with respect to the period of effectiveness of a specific ACTION-TASK.
action-task-timing-day-code OP The specific value that represents the notional start of the ACTION in terms of a day with defined operational meaning.
action-task-timing-hour-code OP The specific value that represents the notional start of the ACTION in terms of an hour with defined operational meaning.
candidate-target-list-id (FK) OP The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific CANDIDATE-TARGET-LIST and to distinguish it from all other CANDIDATE-TARGET-LISTs.
organisation-structure-index (FK) OP The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific ORGANISATION-STRUCTURE for a specific ORGANISATION and to distinguish it from all other ORGANISATION-STRUCTUREs for that ORGANISATION.
organisation-structure-root-organisation-id (FK) OP The organisation-id of a specific ORGANISATION that is the root of the hierarchy for a specific ORGANISATION-STRUCTURE (a role name for object-item-id).
