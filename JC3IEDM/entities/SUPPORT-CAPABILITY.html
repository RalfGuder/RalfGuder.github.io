Entity
Name: SUPPORT-CAPABILITY
Definition: A CAPABILITY, required for planning, of those FACILITYs, MATERIELs and ORGANISATIONs or FACILITY-TYPEs, EQUIPMENT-TYPEs and ORGANISATION-TYPEs that are deemed as having the ability to provide supplies or services.
Supertype: CAPABILITY
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition

support-capability-category-code OP The specific value that represents the class of SUPPORT-CAPABILITY.
support-capability-descriptor-code OP The specific value that represents the SUPPORT-CAPABILITY that is being quantified.
