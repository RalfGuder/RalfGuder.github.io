Entity
Name: SOLID-SURFACE-STATUS
Definition: A GEOGRAPHIC-FEATURE-STATUS that is a record of condition of a specific solid surface.
Supertype: GEOGRAPHIC-FEATURE-STATUS
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
object-item-status-index (PK) (FK) MA The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific OBJECT-ITEM-STATUS for a specific OBJECT-ITEM and to distinguish it from all other OBJECT-ITEM-STATUSs for that OBJECT-ITEM.
solid-surface-status-id (PK) (FK) MA The geographic-feature-id of the GEOGRAPHIC-FEATURE that is the subject of a specific SOLID-SURFACE-STATUS (a role name for object-item-id).
solid-surface-status-code OP The specific value that represents the status of a specific solid surface.
solid-surface-status-demolition-status-code OP The specific value that represents the status of an object destined for demolition.
solid-surface-status-surface-condition-code OP The specific value that represents the physical status of a solid surface area.
solid-surface-status-surface-firmness-code OP The specific value that represents the firmness of a surface area, in terms of its ability to support land vehicles or helicopters.
solid-surface-status-vegetation-category-code OP The specific value that represents the general vegetation class of a specific SOLID-SURFACE-STATUS.
solid-surface-status-vegetation-subcategory-code OP The specific value that represents the detailed vegetation class of a specific SOLID-SURFACE-STATUS.
