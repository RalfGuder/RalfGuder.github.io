Entity
Name: RELATIVE-COORDINATE-SYSTEM
Definition: A rectangular frame of reference defined by an origin, x and y axes in the horizontal plane, and a z-axis. The vertical z-axis is normal to the xy-plane with positive direction determined from the right-hand rule when the x-axis is rotated toward the y-axis.
Subtypes: OBJECT-REFERENCE, POINT-REFERENCE
