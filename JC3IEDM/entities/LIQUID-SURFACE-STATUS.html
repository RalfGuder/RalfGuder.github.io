Entity
Name: LIQUID-SURFACE-STATUS
Definition: A GEOGRAPHIC-FEATURE-STATUS that is a record of condition of a specific liquid surface.
Supertype: GEOGRAPHIC-FEATURE-STATUS
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
liquid-surface-status-id (PK) (FK) MA The geographic-feature-id of the GEOGRAPHIC-FEATURE that is the subject of a specific LIQUID-SURFACE-STATUS (a role name for object-item-id).
object-item-status-index (PK) (FK) MA The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific OBJECT-ITEM-STATUS for a specific OBJECT-ITEM and to distinguish it from all other OBJECT-ITEM-STATUSs for that OBJECT-ITEM.
liquid-surface-status-sea-state-code OP The specific value that represents a range of wave heights on a specific liquid surface.
liquid-surface-status-surface-condition-code OP The specific value that represents the physical status of a liquid surface area.
liquid-surface-status-wave-direction-code OP The specific value that represents the direction of waves in a specific liquid surface.
