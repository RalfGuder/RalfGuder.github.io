Name Opt Definition
approach-direction-id (PK) (FK) MA The control-feature-id of a specific APPROACH-DIRECTION (a role name for object-item-id).
approach-direction-angle-text OP The character string assigned to represent a runway in terms of a whole number nearest one-tenth of the magnetic azimuth of the centreline of the runway, measured clockwise from magnetic north (where six degrees is used as the break off point for the next highest number).
approach-direction-category-code OP The specific value that differentiates between left, right and centre parallel runways, Short Takeoff and Landing (STOL) or true as applicable.
