Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
holding-index (PK) MA The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific HOLDING for a specific OBJECT-ITEM and a specific OBJECT-TYPE and to distinguish it from all other HOLDINGs for that OBJECT-ITEM and that OBJECT-TYPE.
object-item-id (PK) (FK) MA The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific OBJECT-ITEM and to distinguish it from all other OBJECT-ITEMs.
object-type-id (PK) (FK) MA The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific OBJECT-TYPE and to distinguish it from all other OBJECT-TYPEs.
holding-on-hand-quantity OP The numeric value representing the quantity of specific OBJECT-TYPEs physically held on-hand, not including reserves, by a specific OBJECT-ITEM.
holding-operational-count OP The integer value representing the number of specific OBJECT-TYPEs a specific OBJECT-ITEM has available for operations.
holding-required-calculation-method-code OP The specific value that represents the required stocks calculation method used for the count of OBJECT-TYPEs in a specific HOLDING.
holding-required-on-hand-quantity OP The numeric value representing the quantity of specific OBJECT-TYPEs, not including reserves, required to be held on-hand by a specific OBJECT-ITEM in accordance with Force Standards or established mission requirements.
holding-required-total-quantity OP The numeric value representing the total quantity of specific OBJECT-TYPEs required to be held on-hand and in reserve by a specific OBJECT-ITEM to meet the NATO assigned task in accordance with Force Standards or established mission requirements.
holding-total-quantity OP The numeric value representing the total quantity, to include reserves, of specific OBJECT-TYPEs physically held by a specific OBJECT-ITEM. The unit of measure is derived from the OBJECT-TYPE specification.
reporting-data-id (FK) OP The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific REPORTING-DATA and to distinguish it from all other REPORTING-DATAs.
