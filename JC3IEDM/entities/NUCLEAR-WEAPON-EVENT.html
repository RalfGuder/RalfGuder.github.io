Entity
Name: NUCLEAR-WEAPON-EVENT
Definition: A NUCLEAR-EVENT that involves the detonation of a nuclear device.
Supertype: NUCLEAR-EVENT
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
nuclear-weapon-event-id (PK) (FK) MA The nuclear-event-id of a specific NUCLEAR-WEAPON-EVENT (a role name for action-id).
nuclear-weapon-event-crater-presence-code OP The specific value that represents the presence of a crater.
nuclear-weapon-event-crater-width-dimension OP The one-dimensional linear distance representing the width of a crater caused by the detonation of a nuclear device.
nuclear-weapon-event-flash-to-bang-duration OP The numeric value that represents a quantity of time in milliseconds from light being first observed until the sound of the nuclear detonation is heard in a specific NUCLEAR-WEAPON-EVENT.
nuclear-weapon-event-yield-group-code OP The specific value that represents the energy released in the detonation of a nuclear weapon, measured in terms of the kilotons or megatons of trinitrotoluene required to produce the same energy release.
nuclear-weapon-event-yield-quantity OP The numeric value that represents the energy release or predicted to be released by the detonation of a nuclear weapon. The unit of measure is kiloton.
