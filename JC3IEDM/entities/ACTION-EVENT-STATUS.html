Entity
Name: ACTION-EVENT-STATUS
Definition: The perceived appraisal of the actual progress of a specific ACTION-EVENT as determined by the reporting organisation.

Name Opt Definition
action-event-id (PK) (FK) MA The action-id of a specific ACTION-EVENT (a role name for action-id).
action-event-status-index (PK) MA The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific ACTION-EVENT-STATUS for a specific ACTION-EVENT and to distinguish it from all other ACTION-EVENT-STATUSs for that ACTION-EVENT.
