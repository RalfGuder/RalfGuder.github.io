Entity
Name: GEOGRAPHIC-FEATURE-STATUS
Definition: An OBJECT-ITEM-STATUS that is a record of condition of a specific GEOGRAPHIC-FEATURE.
Supertype: OBJECT-ITEM-STATUS
Subtypes: LIQUID-BODY-STATUS, LIQUID-SURFACE-STATUS, SOLID-SURFACE-STATUS
Attributes:
Name Opt Definition
geographic-feature-status-id (PK) (FK) MA The geographic-feature-id of the GEOGRAPHIC-FEATURE that is the subject of a specific GEOGRAPHIC-FEATURE-STATUS (a role name for object-item-id).
object-item-status-index (PK) (FK) MA The unique value, or set of characters, assigned to represent a specific OBJECT-ITEM-STATUS for a specific OBJECT-ITEM and to distinguish it from all other OBJECT-ITEM-STATUSs for that OBJECT-ITEM.
geographic-feature-status-category-code OP The specific value that represents the class of GEOGRAPHIC-FEATURE-STATUS. It serves as a discriminator that partitions GEOGRAPHIC-FEATURE-STATUS into subtypes.
geographic-feature-status-mine-presence-code OP The specific value that indicates whether a specific GEOGRAPHIC-FEATURE contains mines.
geographic-feature-status-surface-recirculation-indicator-code OP The specific value that indicates whether the surface will recirculate as a result of rotor downwash.
