Domain
Name: angle-precision-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the maximum resolution used for the expression of a value of an angle.
Definition Source: MIP-NDAG
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
1/10 degree Angular precision is expressed to the precision of 1/10 of a degree. MIP-NDAG 10DEG 1000004
1/10 minute Angular precision is expressed to the precision of 1/10 of a minute. MIP-NDAG 10MN 1000005
1/10 second Angular precision is expressed to the precision of 1/10 of a second. MIP-NDAG 10SEC 1000006
1/100 minute Angular precision is expressed to the precision of 1/100 of a minute (centiminute). MIP-NDAG 100MN 1000002
1/100 second Angular precision is expressed to the precision of 1/100 of a second. MIP-NDAG 100SEC 1000003
1/1000 minute Angular precision is expressed to the precision of 1/1000 of a minute. MIP-NDAG 1000MN 1000001
Degree Angular precision is expressed to the precision of a degree (60 minutes). MIP-NDAG DEGREE 1000007
Mil Angular precision is expressed to the precision of 1 mil (1/6400 of a full circle). MIP-NDAG MIL 1000008
Minute Angular precision is expressed to the precision of a minute (60 seconds). MIP-NDAG MINUTE 1000009
Second Angular precision is expressed to the precision of a second. MIP-NDAG SECOND 1000010
