Domain
Name: organisation-status-operational-status-qualifier-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the qualification of the operational status of a specific ORGANISATION.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Destroyed Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that an ORGANISATION is not, and not expected ever to be, capable of performing the missions or functions for which it is created. ATCCIS adapted AAP-6(T) (Operational Readiness) DSTRYD 1000001
Heavily damaged Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation to be heavily damaged. ATCCIS HVYDAM 1000002
Lacking vital resources Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that an ORGANISATION is deficient or lacking of some mission-critical resources (e.g., fuel, ammunition). ATCCIS LVR 1000003
Lightly damaged Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation to be only lightly damaged. ATCCIS LGTDAM 1000004
Lost Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a specific organisation is missing under unknown circumstances. ATCCIS LST 1000005
Moderately damaged Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation to be moderately damaged. ATCCIS MODDAM 1000006
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000007

Entity Attribute Opt
