Domain
Name: action-task-timing-hour-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the notional start of the ACTION in terms of an hour with defined operational meaning.
Definition Source: MIP-NDAG
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
F The time designated for the start of cross-FLOT aviation operations. STANAG 2014 Ed 9 F 1000001
G The time on which an order (normally national) is given to deploy a unit. Adapted STANAG 2014 Ed 9 G 1000002
H 1. The specific time at which an operation or exercise commences or is due to commence. It is also the time at which the Line of Departure is crossed by the leading element in an attack. 2. In amphibious operations, the time at which the first waterborne wave of an amphibious assault lands on a beach. STANAG 2014 Ed 9 H 1000003
K The time on which a convoy system is introduced or is due to be introduced on any particular land convoy route or sea convoy lane. Adapted STANAG 2014 Ed 9 K 1000004
L In amphibious or airmobile operations, the time at which the first helicopter of the heliborne assault wave touches down in the landing zone (LZ). STANAG 2014 Ed 9 L 1000005
P In airborne operations, the time at which the lead parachute element is to arrive over the parachute impact point to begin operations. STANAG 2014 Ed 9 P 1000006
T The time of Transfer of Authority. Adapted STANAG 2014 Ed 9 T 1000007
Y In airmobile operations the time at which the first helicopter in the first wave departs the "Pick-up-Point"(PUP). STANAG 2014 Ed 9 Y 1000008
