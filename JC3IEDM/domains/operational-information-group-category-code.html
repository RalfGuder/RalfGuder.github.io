Domain
Name: operational-information-group-category-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the class of OPERATIONAL-INFORMATION-GROUP.
Definition Source: MIP-NDAG
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Correlated enemy and unknown The specific OPERATIONAL-INFORMATION-GROUP represents aggregated data about opposing or unknown force elements. Both factual reports and estimates may be included as part of the OPERATIONAL-INFORMATION-GROUP. MIP-NDAG CORENU 1000002
Friendly and neutral (non-organisational) The specific OPERATIONAL-INFORMATION-GROUP represents data about objects, such as facilities and control measures, that a given organisation controls or is responsible for directly. MIP-NDAG FRNENO 1000004
Friendly and neutral (organisational) The specific OPERATIONAL-INFORMATION-GROUP represents essential data about friendly and neutral organisations. The data set includes as a minimum holdings, status, and location. MIP-NDAG FRDNEU 1000003
Globally significant The specific OPERATIONAL-INFORMATION-GROUP represents data that cannot be aggregated and does not fall within the scope of other predefined subcategories. MIP-NDAG GLBSGN 1000005
Plans/Orders The specific OPERATIONAL-INFORMATION-GROUP represents planning and/or order data that is to be distributed as a package. MIP-NDAG PLNORD 1000008
Uncorrelated enemy and unknown The specific OPERATIONAL-INFORMATION-GROUP represents factual data about individual detection or report of opposing or unknown force elements without the data being aggregated. MIP-NDAG UNCORR 1000007
