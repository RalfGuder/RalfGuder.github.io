Definition Source: MIP-NDAG
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Air-defence A weapon specifically designed for the engagement of aircraft. AIntP-3 - Def adapted OED AD 1000001
Anti-submarine A weapon specifically designed for the engagement of submarines. AIntP-3 - Def adapted OED ANTSUB 1000012
Anti-tank A weapon specifically designed for the engagement of armoured vehicles. AIntP-3 - Def adapted OED AT 1000002
Cannon A weapon for firing projectiles, having a heavy metal tube installed on a carriage or mounting. Adapted OED CANNON 1000003
Field artillery Artillery that is self-propelled or towed for use in the field in support of manoeuvre forces. MIP FA 1000004
Missile system A system designed to launch one or more powered guided objects at a target. Adapted OED MISSYS 1000005
Mortar A portable, muzzle-loading cannon used to fire shells at low velocities, short ranges, and high trajectories. AIntP-3 - Def adapted OED MORTAR 1000006
Rocket artillery A system designed to launch one or more powered unguided objects at a target. Adapted AAP-6 RCKART 1000007
Small arms Individual portable weapon for defensive purposes. AIntP-3 - Def adapted OED SMARMS 1000008
Tank An armoured vehicle whose principal weapon is a direct fire gun optimised for the destruction of armoured vehicles. AIntP-3 - Def adapted OED TANK 1000009
