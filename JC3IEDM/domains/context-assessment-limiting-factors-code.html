Domain
Name: context-assessment-limiting-factors-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the logistic factors, which are degrading operational capability in a specific CONTEXT-ASSESSMENT.
Definition Source: Adapted ADatP-3 (Bi-SC Reporting directive 80-3, Volume V, Section 2)
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Cross-servicing capability The specific CONTEXT-ASSESSMENT reflects cross-servicing capability limitations. Bi-SC Reporting directive 80-3 CROSS 1000001
Equipment limitations The specific CONTEXT-ASSESSMENT reflects equipment limitations affecting direct mission support. Bi-SC Reporting directive 80-3 EQPLMT 1000002
Exceptional supply The specific CONTEXT-ASSESSMENT reflects exceptional supply shortages affecting the unit's mission. Bi-SC Reporting directive 80-3 EXPSPL 1000003
Facility limitations The specific CONTEXT-ASSESSMENT reflects facility limitations affecting direct mission support. Bi-SC Reporting directive 80-3 FACLMT 1000004
Maintenance capability The specific CONTEXT-ASSESSMENT reflects maintenance capability limitations, including battle damage repair information. Bi-SC Reporting directive 80-3 MNTCAP 1000005
Medical limitations The specific CONTEXT-ASSESSMENT reflects medical limitations affecting the unit's mission. Bi-SC Reporting directive 80-3 MEDLMT 1000006
Munitions capability The specific CONTEXT-ASSESSMENT reflects munitions capability limitations - capability for build-up, storage and transportation, not holdings. Bi-SC Reporting directive 80-3 MUNCAP 1000007
No change The specific CONTEXT-ASSESSMENT reflects no change limitations since last report. Bi-SC Reporting directive 80-3 NOCHNG 1000008
No limitations The specific CONTEXT-ASSESSMENT reflects no limitations since last report. MIP-NDAG NOLMTN 1000009
Personnel limitations The specific CONTEXT-ASSESSMENT reflects personnel limitations in any area. Bi-SC Reporting directive 80-3 PRSLMT 1000010
Petroleum, oil and lubrication The specific CONTEXT-ASSESSMENT reflects petroleum, oil and lubrication capability limitations - capability to store and deliver, not holdings. Bi-SC Reporting directive 80-3 POL 1000011
Rapid runway repair capability The specific CONTEXT-ASSESSMENT reflects capability limitations to perform rapid runway repair. Bi-SC Reporting directive 80-3 RRRCAP 1000012
Not otherwise specified The appropriate value is not in the set of specified values. ATCCIS NOS 1000013
