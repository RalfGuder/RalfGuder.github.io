Domain
Name: organisation-status-fire-mode-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the status of weapons employment constraint for a specific ORGANISATION.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Hold fire An emergency order to stop firing; missiles in flight must be prevented from intercepting, if technically possible. Adapted AAP-6 V 2005 HLDFIR 1000001
Weapons free A weapon control order imposing a status whereby weapons systems may be fired at any target not positively identified as friendly. Adapted AAP-6 V 2005 WPNFRE 1000002
Weapons hold A weapon control order imposing a status whereby weapons systems may only be fired in self defence or in response to a formal order. Adapted AAP-6 V 2005 WPNHLD 1000005
Weapons tight A weapon control order imposing a status whereby weapons systems may be fired only at targets recognised as hostile. Adapted AAP-6 V 2005 WPNTGT 1000003
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000004
Usage:
Entity Attribute Opt
