Domain
Name: context-reporting-data-association-category-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the type of relation of a specific CONTEXT with a specific REPORTING-DATA.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Implies A relationship between a REPORTING-DATA and a CONTEXT that is the logical consequence of the CONTEXT. ATCCIS IMPL 1000001
Is a correction of A relationship between a REPORTING-DATA and a CONTEXT with the purpose of amending the CONTEXT. ATCCIS CORR 1000002
Is confirmed by A relationship between a REPORTING-DATA and a CONTEXT in which the data referenced by REPORTING-DATA affirms the data referenced by CONTEXT. ATCCIS CONF 1000003
Is defined to be A relationship between a REPORTING-DATA and a CONTEXT in which a CONTEXT is recorded as a REPORTING-DATA. ATCCIS ISDFT 1000006
Is negated by A relationship between a REPORTING-DATA and a CONTEXT, where the information referred to by the REPORTING-DATA is in any way contradictory to the information referred to by the CONTEXT. ATCCIS NEGA 1000004
Is superseded by A relationship between a REPORTING-DATA and a CONTEXT, where the information referred to by the REPORTING-DATA replaces the information referred to by the CONTEXT. ATCCIS SUPR 1000005
