Domain
Name: atmosphere-inversion-layer-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the height of the inversion layer in the atmosphere. The stability class describes the degree of mixing of released material in the atmosphere.
Definition Source: CBRN COI
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
A Top of inversion layer lower than 800 metres above ground. ATP-45(B) and ADatP-3 A 1000001
B Top of inversion layer lower than 400 metres above ground. ATP-45(B) and ADatP-3 B 1000002
C Top of inversion layer lower than 200 metres above ground. ATP-45(B) and ADatP-3 C 1000003
