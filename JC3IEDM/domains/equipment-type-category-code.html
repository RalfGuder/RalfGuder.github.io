Domain
Name: equipment-type-category-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the class of EQUIPMENT-TYPE.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
AIRCRAFT-TYPE An EQUIPMENT-TYPE that is designed to fly. ATCCIS AIRCFT 1000001
CBRN-EQUIPMENT-TYPE An EQUIPMENT-TYPE that is designed for specialised roles in detecting, decontaminating or reconnoitring NBC (CBRN) materiel. CBRN COI CBRNEQ 1000006
ELECTRONIC-EQUIPMENT-TYPE An EQUIPMENT-TYPE that is designed to use electronic processing to realise its primary function. ATCCIS ELCTRN 1000002
ENGINEERING-EQUIPMENT-TYPE An EQUIPMENT-TYPE that is designed to accomplish engineering functions. ATCCIS ENGEQ 1000003
MARITIME-EQUIPMENT-TYPE An EQUIPMENT-TYPE that is designed to be used in a maritime environment. MIP-NDAG MRTMEQ 1000010
MISCELLANEOUS-EQUIPMENT-TYPE An EQUIPMENT-TYPE whose designed function does not fit in any other defined category. ATCCIS MISCEQ 1000005
RAILCAR-TYPE An EQUIPMENT-TYPE that is designed to operate on rail tracks. ATCCIS RAIL 1000007
VEHICLE-TYPE An EQUIPMENT-TYPE that is designed to operate on land routes (other than rail) with a primary role of transporting personnel, equipment or supplies. ATCCIS VEHCLE 1000008
VESSEL-TYPE An EQUIPMENT-TYPE that is designed to operate on or under the water surface. ATCCIS VESSEL 1000009
WEAPON-TYPE An EQUIPMENT-TYPE of any kind used in warfare or combat to attack and overcome an enemy. MIP-NDAG WEPTYP 1000004
Usage:
Entity Attribute Opt
