Domain
Name: nuclear-yield-group-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the explosive yield of a nuclear weapon that is the amount of energy discharged when the weapon is detonated, expressed in the equivalent mass of trinitrotoluene (TNT), either in kilotons (thousands of tons of TNT) or megatons (millions of tons of TNT).
Definition Source: CBRN COI
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
ALFA Yield Group ALFA is less than 2 KT. ATP-45(B) ALFA 1000001
BRAVO Yield Group BRAVO is 2 KT to less than 5 KT. ATP-45(B) BRAVO 1000002
CHARLIE Yield Group CHARLIE is 5 KT to less than 30 KT. ATP-45(B) CHARLI 1000003
DELTA Yield Group DELTA is 30 KT to less than 100 KT. ATP-45(B) DELTA 1000004
ECHO Yield Group ECHO is 100 KT to less than 300 KT. ATP-45(B) ECHO 1000005
FOXTROT Yield Group FOXTROT is 300 KT to less than 1000 KT. ATP-45(B) FOXTRT 1000006
GOLF Yield Group GOLF is 1000 KT to less than 3000 KT. ATP-45(B) GOLF 1000007
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000008
Not otherwise specified The appropriate value is not in the set of specified values. ATCCIS NOS 1000009
