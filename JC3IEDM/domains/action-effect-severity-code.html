Domain
Name: action-effect-severity-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the degree of seriousness of a specific ACTION-EFFECT.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Minor disruption A subjective judgement by the reporting organisation that the specific ACTION resulted in minor disorder with respect to the object or class that is the focus of the ACTION-EFFECT. ATCCIS MINDSR 1000002
No disruption A subjective judgement by the reporting organisation that the specific ACTION resulted in no disorder with respect to the object or class that is the focus of the ACTION-EFFECT. ATCCIS NODSRP 1000001
Severe disruption A subjective judgement by the reporting organisation that the specific ACTION resulted in severe disorder with respect to the object or class that is the focus of the ACTION-EFFECT. ATCCIS SEVDSR 1000003
Total disruption A subjective judgement by the reporting organisation that the specific ACTION resulted in complete disorder with respect to the object or class that is the focus of the ACTION-EFFECT. ATCCIS TOTDSR 1000004
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000005
Usage:
Entity Attribute Opt
ACTION-EFFECT action-effect-severity-code OP
