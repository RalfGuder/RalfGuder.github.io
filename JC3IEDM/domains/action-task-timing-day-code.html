Domain
Name: action-task-timing-day-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the notional start of the ACTION in terms of a day with defined operational meaning.
Definition Source: MIP-NDAG
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
C The day on which deployment for an operation commences or is due to commence. STANAG 2014 Ed 9 C 1000001
D The day on which an operation commences or is due to commence. This may be commencement of hostilities or any other operation. STANAG 2014 Ed 9 D 1000002
E The day on which a NATO exercise commences. STANAG 2014 Ed 9 E 1000003
G The day on which an order (normally national) is given to deploy a unit. Adapted STANAG 2014 Ed 9 G 1000004
J French equivalent to D-day. STANAG 2014 Ed 9 J 1000005
K The day on which a convoy system is introduced or is due to be introduced on any particular land convoy route or sea convoy lane. Adapted STANAG 2014 Ed 9 K 1000006
M The day on which mobilization commences or is due to commence. STANAG 2014 Ed 9 M 1000007
T The day of Transfer of Authority. Adapted STANAG 2014 Ed 9 T 1000008
