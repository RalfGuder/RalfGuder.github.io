Name: subsurface-vessel-type-category-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the class of subsurface vessel.
Definition Source: MCCIS, Maritime Command and Control Information System
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Deep submergence vehicle Submersible designed to operate at great depth (more than 300 metres). STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 DSV 1000001
Deep submergence vehicle, military Military operated submersible designed to operate at great depth (more than 300 metres). STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 YSV 1000002
Deep submersible rescue vehicle (military) Military operated submersible designed for submarine rescue operations at great depth (more than 300 metres) and military operated. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 YSRV 1000003
Submarine, attack, guided missile Submarine fitted with underwater to surface or surface-to-surface missiles. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 SSG 1000004
Submarine, attack, guided missile, nuclear Submarine fitted with underwater to surface or surface-to-surface missiles with nuclear power. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 SSGN 1000005
Submarine, attack, nuclear Nuclear propelled attack submarine with both anti-submarine and anti-surface capability. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 SSN 1000006
Submarine, auxiliary Submarine used mainly in a non-combatant role which has at least a residual combat capability. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 SSA 1000007
Submarine, auxiliary, nuclear powered Submarine used mainly in a non-combatant role that has at least a residual combat capability and with nuclear power. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 SSAN 1000008
Submarine, ballistic missile A subsurface vessel designed to deliver ballistic missile attacks against assigned targets from either a submerged or surfaced condition. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 SSB 1000009
Submarine, ballistic missile, nuclear A subsurface vessel designed to deliver ballistic missile attacks against assigned targets from either a submerged or surfaced condition with nuclear power. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 SSBN 1000010
Submarine, coastal Submarine designed primarily for operations in coastal and shallow waters. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 SSC 1000011
Submarine, general General designator for a warship, capable of operating under water and usually equipped with torpedoes, missiles and a periscope. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 SS 1000012
Submarine, midget, swimmer Midget submarine or submersible designed primarily for special operations and operated by naval forces. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 SSW 1000013
Submarine, military capability unknown Submarine whose full military capability is unknown or has not been determined. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 SSU 1000014
Submarine, military capability unknown, nuclear powered Submarine with nuclear power whose full military capability is unknown or has not been determined. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 SSUN 1000015
Submarine, patrol Long-range patrol submarine. May have primary anti- surface or anti-submarine role. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 SSK 1000016
Submarine, training Submarine used primarily in a training role but with at least a residual combat capability. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 SST 1000017
Submersible, general (commercial) General designator for non-combatant submersible. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 TS 1000018
Submersible, rescue (commercial) Submersible designed specifically for underwater rescue operations. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 TSR 1000019
Submersible, rescue, military Military operated submersible designed specifically for underwater rescue operations. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 YSG 1000020
Submersible, research (commercial) Submersible with no combat capability used in research role. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 TSG 1000021
Submersible, research, military Military operated submersible with no combat capability used in research role. STANAG 1166 Ed 6 2 Oct 2000 YSR 1000022
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000023
Not otherwise specified The appropriate value is not in the set of specified values. ATCCIS NOS 1000024
