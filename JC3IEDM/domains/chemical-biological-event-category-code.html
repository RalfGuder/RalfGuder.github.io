Domain
Name: chemical-biological-event-category-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the class of CHEMICAL-BIOLOGICAL-EVENT.
Definition Source: CBRN COI
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Biological alarm An action by which a biological detector is triggered or a group is warned. CBRN COI BIOALM 1000001
Biological attack Employing the use of biological materiel(s) [agent(s)] to kill, injure, or incapacitate, for a significant period of time, man or animals, and deny or hinder the use of areas, facilities or materiel, or defence against such employment. Adapted ATP-45(B) BIOATT 1000002
Biological release other than attack (ROTA) The release of a biological agent or toxin into the environment intentionally or accidentally but not for the intended purpose of conducting an attack. CBRN COI BIROTA 1000003
Biological sampling The action of detecting a biological contaminant. CBRN COI BIOSMP 1000004
Chemical alarm An action by which a chemical detector is triggered or a group is warned. CBRN COI CHMALM 1000005
Chemical attack Employing the use of chemical materiel(s) [agent(s)] to produce casualties in man or animals and damage to plants or materiel; or defence against such employment. Adapted ATP-45(B) CHMATT 1000006
Chemical release other than attack (ROTA) The release of chemicals or chemical agents into the environment intentionally or accidentally but not for the intended purpose of conducting an attack. CBRN COI CHROTA 1000007
Chemical sampling The action of detecting a chemical contaminant. CBRN COI CHMSMP 1000008
Chemical/biological facility event An event that involves viable agent released to the atmosphere following a strike against a facility containing chemical warfare agent(s) and/or biological warfare agent(s). CBRN COI CBFAC 1000009
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000010
