Name: group-characteristic-age-group-code
Definition: The specific value that identifies the age group in a specific GROUP-CHARACTERISTIC.
Definition Source: MIP
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Adult The group includes ages from 13 through 60 years. MIP ADULT 1000001
Child The group includes ages less than 13 years. MIP CHILD 1000002
Elderly The group includes ages over 60 years. MIP ELDRLY 1000003
Mixed The group includes a mixture of ages. MIP MIXED 1000004
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000005
