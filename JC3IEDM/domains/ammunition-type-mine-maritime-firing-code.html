Name: ammunition-type-mine-maritime-firing-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the firing mechanism for a maritime mine.
Definition Source: MIP-NDAG/MWTU
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Acoustic A (maritime) mine firing mechanism with an acoustic circuit which responds to the acoustic field of a ship or sweep. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted ACOUST 1000003
Acoustic audio frequency A (maritime) mine firing mechanism with an acoustic audio circuit which responds to the acoustic field of a ship or sweep. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted ACOUAF 1000004
Acoustic high frequency A (maritime) mine firing mechanism with an acoustic high frequency circuit, 20 to 50 Hz, which responds to the acoustic field of a ship or sweep. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted ACOUHF 1000005
Acoustic low frequency A (maritime) mine firing mechanism with an acoustic low frequency circuit, less than 2 Hz, which responds to the acoustic field of a ship or sweep. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted ACOULF 1000006
Active A (maritime) mine firing mechanism actuated by the reflection from a target of a signal emitted by the mine. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted ACTIVE 1000001
Antenna A contact (maritime) mine firing mechanism which, when touched by a ferrous object, set up galvanic action to fire the mine. The antenna generally takes the form of a special section in the mooring cable, and/or special cable suspended above the mine by a float. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted ANTENA 1000007
Coarse, anti-sweep A (maritime) mine firing mechanism fitted that is regarded as coarse firing. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted COARAS 1000008
Combination, overlap A (maritime) mine firing mechanism that fires depending upon the previous simultaneous or subsequent reception of signals resulting from other influences, such as acoustic and pressure. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted COMBIN 1000009
Contact A (maritime) mine firing mechanism that is designed to fire by physical contact between the target mine or its appendages. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted CONTCT 1000010
Fitted with delayed arming or rising mechanism A (maritime) mine firing mechanism fitted with a timing mechanism that keeps the mine circuits open (off) for a preset time after laying. If fitted with also a rising mechanism the release and rising of the mine can be delayed by means of a device fitted to either the sinker or to the windings of the mooring cable. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted FITWDA 1000012
Fitted with ship counter A (maritime) mine firing mechanism fitted with a ship counting mechanism, a tactical device. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted FITWSC 1000011
Influence A (maritime) mine firing mechanism actuated by the effect of a target on some physical condition in the vicinity of the mine or on radiations emanating from the mine. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted INFLUN 1000002
Magnetic A (maritime) mine firing mechanism with a magnetic influence circuit that responds to the magnetic field of a ship. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted MAGNET 1000013
Magnetic H, horizontal component A (maritime) mine firing mechanism with a magnetic influence circuit that responds to the horizontal component of the magnetic field of a ship. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted MAGNHH 1000014
Magnetic T, total component A (maritime) mine with a magnetic influence circuit that responds to the total component of the magnetic field of a ship. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted MAGNTT 1000015
Magnetic V, vertical component A (maritime) mine firing mechanism with a magnetic influence circuit which responds to the vertical component of the magnetic field of a ship. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted MAGNVV 1000016
Minehunting sonar decoy A small object with a large sonar signal, or inert minelike objects, Non-mine Bottom Objects (NOMBOs). ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted MINHSD 1000017
Multi-look mines A type of maritime mine actuation. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def MTP-6 Vol 1 MULTLM 1000018
Passive A (maritime) mine firing mechanism that does not emit a signal to detect the presence of a target. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted PASIVE 1000019
Pressure A (maritime) mine firing mechanism with a pressure sensitive circuit which responds to the pressure variation as a result of a ship or sweep. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted PRESUR 1000020
Sensitive for normal target A (maritime) mine firing mechanism that may actuated by a small change in its influence field. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted SENFNT 1000021
Sequence A (maritime) mine firing mechanism that has a firing circuit which requires actuation by a predetermined sequence of influences of predetermined magnitude. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted SEQUEN 1000022
Very sensitive, anti sweeper A (maritime) mine firing mechanism that is fitted with a sensitive firing mechanism that react to a small influences of pressure, acoustic or magnetic (for magnetic values of less than 200 nanotesla are sufficient.) ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1601/1 - Def ATP-6(C) Vol 1 adapted VERSEN 1000023
