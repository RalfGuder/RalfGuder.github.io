Name: capability-day-night-code
Definition: The specific value that defines the light conditions that apply to a particular CAPABILITY.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Day The figures are based on the capability being invoked during daylight hours. ATCCIS DAY 1000001
Day and night The figures are based on the capability being invoked at any time. ATCCIS DN 1000002
Night The figures are based on the capability being invoked during hours of darkness. ATCCIS N 1000003
