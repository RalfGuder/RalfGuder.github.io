Domain
Name: reporting-data-accuracy-code
Definition: The specific value that represents, for intelligence purpose, the general appraisal of the subject matter in graded terms to indicate the extent or degree to which it has been judged to be free from mistake or error or to conform to truth or some recognised standard value.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Confirmed Reported data is confirmed by at least 3 independent sources. AIntP-3 1 1000001
Doubtful Reported data can be viewed with suspicion. AIntP-3 4 1000004
Improbable Reported data shall be considered as probably erroneous. AIntP-3 5 1000005
Possible Reported data is given by only one source. AIntP-3 3 1000003
Probable Reported data is confirmed by 2 independent sources. AIntP-3 2 1000002
Truth cannot be judged Basis for the estimate of Reported data cannot be estimated. AIntP-3 6 1000006
