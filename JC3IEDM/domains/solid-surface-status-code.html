Domain
Name: solid-surface-status-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the status of a specific solid surface.
Definition Source: MIP-NDAG
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Cleared A status indicating that terrain is passable or traversable. MIP-NDAG CLEARD 1000001
Contaminated Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation to be defiled, sullied or infected by contact with toxic substances. MIP-NDAG CNTMND 1000008
Destroyed Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation to be rendered useless or ineffective. ATCCIS adapted AAP-6(T) (Operational Readiness) DSTRYD 1000003
Heavily damaged Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation to be heavily damaged. ATCCIS HVYDAM 1000004
Lightly damaged Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation to be only lightly damaged. ATCCIS LGTDAM 1000005
Moderately damaged Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation to be moderately damaged. ATCCIS MODDAM 1000006
Obstructed Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that the terrain is obstructed. MIP-NDAG OBSTRD 1000009
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000007

Entity Attribute Opt
