Domain
Name: facility-status-operational-status-qualifier-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the qualification of the operational status of a specific FACILITY.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Breached Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a FACILITY has been penetrated. Adapted APP-9 BRCHED 1000010
Burned out Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a FACILITY has been set on fire and is destroyed. ATCCIS BRNOUT 1000015
Covered by fire Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a FACILITY is being protected by weaponry. Adapted APP-9 CVRFIR 1000011
Denied Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a FACILITY is unavailable through means such as removal, contamination or erection of obstructions. ATCCIS adapted AAP-6(T) DENIED 1000001
Destroyed Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a FACILITY is not, and not expected ever to be, capable of performing the missions or functions for which it is organised or designed. ATCCIS adapted AAP-6(T) (Operational Readiness) DSTRYD 1000002
Disassembled Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a FACILITY is taken apart in a way that it can be reassembled. ATCCIS DISASM 1000016
Heavily damaged Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation to be heavily damaged. ATCCIS HVYDAM 1000003
In maintenance Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a FACILITY is undergoing service of some kind. ATCCIS INMNT 1000017
Lacking vital resources Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a FACILITY is deficient or lacking of some mission-critical resources (e.g., fuel, ammunition). ATCCIS LVR 1000004
Lightly damaged Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation to be only lightly damaged. ATCCIS LGTDAM 1000005
Lost Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a FACILITY is missing or captured. ATCCIS LST 1000006
Marked Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a FACILITY has been identified. Adapted APP-9 MARKED 1000012
Moderately damaged Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation to be moderately damaged. ATCCIS MODDAM 1000007
Partly dismantled Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a FACILITY is taken apart to some extent. ATCCIS PRTDSM 1000018
Passable Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a FACILITY can be crossed. Adapted APP-9 PASABL 1000013
Prepared for execution Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a FACILITY has been made ready to assume a particular role. Adapted APP-9 PRPEXE 1000014
Sterilized Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a FACILITY has been made incapable of functioning productively or effectively. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1205/1 - Def ATP-6 (c) Vol 1 adapted STERLZ 1000019
Under construction Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a FACILITY is being built. APP-6A OCT 98 UNCNST 1000009
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000008
