Domain
Name: cbrn-event-subcategory-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the detailed class or nature of activity prescribed by CBRN-EVENT.
Definition Source: CBRN COI
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Air burst An explosion of a bomb or projectile above the surface as distinguished from an explosion on contact with the surface or after penetration. ATP-45(B) ARBRST 1000001
Air sampling The operational action to confirm the presence of a gaseous contaminant. ATCCIS AIRSMP 1000002
Alpha radiation source detection The action performed to detect a source of Alpha radiation. ATCCIS ALPRAD 1000003
Beta radiation source detection The action performed to detect a source of Beta radiation. ATCCIS BETRAD 1000004
Burning Fire An event where a fire is consuming materiel and emitting smoke, which may be toxic. ATCCIS BRNFIR 1000005
Continuous flow from damaged pipe or container An event where a continuous flow of possibly toxic liquid is spilled from a container. ATCCIS CNTFLW 1000006
Gamma radiation source detection The action performed to detect a source of Gamma radiation. ATCCIS GAMRAD 1000007
Large quantity of spill liquid An event where a large quantity of possibly toxic liquid is spilled. ATCCIS LRGSPL 1000008
Liquid sampling The operational action to confirm the presence of a liquid contaminant. ATCCIS LQDSMP 1000009
Neutron radiation source detection The action performed to detect a source of Neutron particle. ATCCIS NEUTRN 1000010
Sampling and identification of radiological agent sample The forensic expertise performed to confirm to commanders the actual occurrence of a Nuclear event. ATCCIS SIRA 1000012
Sampling/identification of biological/chemical agent sample The forensic expertise performed to confirm to commanders the actual occurrence of a chemical or biological event. ATCCIS SIBCA 1000011
Small quantity of spill liquid An event where a small quantity of possibly toxic liquid is spilled. ATCCIS SMLSPL 1000013
Sub surface burst The explosion of a nuclear weapon in which the centre of the detonation lies at a point beneath the surface of the ground. ATP-45(B) SBSRBU 1000014
Surface burst An explosion of a nuclear weapon at the surface of land or water; or above the surface at a height less than the maximum radius of the fireball. ATP-45(B) SURBUR 1000015
Visible NBC cloud The action of detecting a visible NBC (CBRN) cloud. CBRN COI CBRNCL 1000016
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000018
Not otherwise specified The appropriate value is not in the set of specified values. ATCCIS NOS 1000017
