Domain
Name: control-feature-status-investigation-status-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the investigation status of the site encompassed by a specific CONTROL-FEATURE.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Investigated, negative There was a formal examination or study on the site associated to the specific control-feature, denying the reason of the investigation. ATCCIS INVNEG 1000001
Investigated, positive There was a formal examination or study on the site associated to the specific control-feature, confirming the reason of the investigation. ATCCIS INVPOS 1000002
Investigation denied The authority controlling the site associated to the specific control-feature did not allow a formal examination or study on it. MIP DENIED 1000006
None There has been no formal examination or study on the site associated to the specific control-feature. ATCCIS NONE 1000003
Under investigation A formal examination or study is been taken on the site associated to the specific control feature. ATCCIS UNDINV 1000004
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000005

Entity Attribute Opt
