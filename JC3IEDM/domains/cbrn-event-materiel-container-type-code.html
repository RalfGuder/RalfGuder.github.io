Domain
Name: cbrn-event-materiel-container-type-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the type of container that stores the materiel (agent) involved in a specific CBRN-EVENT and characterised in ATP-45.
Definition Source: AEP/ATP-45(B)
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Bomb A case filled with explosive, inflammable material, poison gas, or smoke, etc., dropped from aircraft, or thrown or deposited by hand. APP-6A OCT 98 BOM 1000001
Bomblets A small munition capable of containing a biological warfare agent; a submunition. Numerous bomblets could be packed inside a larger munition (e.g., a bomb or warhead) that would explode in the air scattering the bomblets over a relatively wide area. Glossary of Terms for NBC Agents and Defense Equipment BML 1000002
Bunker No definition provided in ATP-45(B). ATP-45(B) BUK 1000003
Generator (aerosol) An instrument or machine capable of releasing a substance as a fine spray by subjecting it to pressure. CBRN COI GEN 1000004
Generic storage container A receptacle in which material is held or carried. Adapted OED CON 1000005
Mine (NBC filled only) A mine containing a chemical or radiological agent designed to kill, injure, or incapacitate personnel or to contaminate materiel or terrain. Adapted NATO Glossary of Terms and Definitions MNE 1000006
Missile A weapon that is self-propelled or directed by remote control, carrying conventional or nuclear explosive. COD MSL 1000007
Nominal 200 litre storage drum A cylindrical container that holds 200 litres. CBRN COI DRM 1000008
Pressurized gas bottle A large metal cylinder holding liquefied gas under pressure. Adapted COD BTL 1000010
Reactor A facility that contains a controlled nuclear fission chain reaction. It can be used to generate electricity, conduct research, and produce isotopes and manmade elements such as plutonium. Glossary of Terms for NBC Agents and Defense Equipment RCT 1000009
Rocket A cylindrical projectile that can be propelled to a great height or distance by the combustion of its contents. COD RKT 1000011
Shell An explosive artillery projectile or bomb. COD SHL 1000012
Spray (tank) A container used to dispense chemical or biological agents. APP-9 - Def CBRN COI SPR 1000013
Stockpile No definition provided in ATP-45(B). ATP-45(B) STK 1000014
Tank A large receptacle or storage chamber, especially for liquid or gas. COD TNK 1000015
Torpedo A cigar-shaped self-propelled underwater missile designed to be fired from a ship, submarine, or an aircraft. COD TOR 1000016
Waste A container of wastes that includes materials such as laboratory wastes and protective clothing. Adapted Glossary of Terms for NBC Agents and Defense Equipment WST 1000017
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000018
