Domain
Name: person-status-duty-status-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the availability of a specific PERSON for duty at a military or civilian post of employment.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Absent Not present at the place of duty for an as yet unspecified reason and has not been posted as either deceased or missing. ATCCIS ABS 1000001
Arrested Being held by friendly forces military police or civilian police force on suspicion of having committed an offence against either military or civil law. ATCCIS ARR 1000003
Assumed killed in action Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that due to the nature of the destruction of the equipment, the individual is assumed dead. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1376/1 adapted - Def NDAG AKIA 1000014
At duty Present at the place of duty. ATCCIS ADU 1000002
Deceased A status indicating that a PERSON is dead. ATCCIS DEC 1000004
Deserted The PERSON has left the place of duty permanently without permission. MIP-NDAG DESRTD 1000013
Hospitalised A status indicating that a PERSON is in a hospital. ATCCIS HSP 1000005
Hostage Has been captured by opposing forces or a terrorist organisation and is being held for the purpose of negotiation. ATCCIS HST 1000006
Missing Is missing but has not been posted as absent or deceased. ATCCIS MIS 1000007
On leave A status indicating that a PERSON is absent from duty with permission. ATCCIS OLV 1000010
Prisoner of war Has been captured by opposing forces and is being held in internment. ATCCIS POW 1000011
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000008
