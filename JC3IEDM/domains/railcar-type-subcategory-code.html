Domain
Name: railcar-type-subcategory-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the detailed class of RAILCAR-TYPE.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Locomotive, diesel A locomotive that uses diesel engine(s) as its source of tractive power. Adapted OCBRH LCMDSL 1000001
Locomotive, diesel/electric A locomotive that uses diesel engine(s) to produce electricity for electric engine(s) that provide the engine with its source of tractive power. Adapted OCBRH LCMDSE 1000002
Locomotive, electric A locomotive that uses electric engine(s) as its source of tractive power, the electricity being supplied from an external source, typically via a pantograph (overhead electrical cable). Adapted OCBRH LCMELC 1000003
Locomotive, steam A locomotive that uses the principle of heating water above its boiling point to produce steam, the expansion in a sealed vessel the pressure becomes the engine(s) source of tractive power. Adapted OCBRH LCMSTM 1000004
Locomotive, tender A locomotive tender is used to carry coal and water for a steam locomotive when not integral to the locomotive design. Adapted OCBRH LCMTND 1000005
Wagon, ISO container(s) Rolling stock used to move standard ISO containers, either single or multiple containers. Adapted OCBRH WGNISO 1000015
Wagon, articulated vehicle transporter Rolling stock specifically designed to carry an articulated truck by rail (for example EUROSTAR). Adapted OCBRH WGNART 1000006
Wagon, brake Rolling stock used to assist with the control of a formed train, that when used are manned and contain auxiliary braking apparatus. Adapted OCBRH WGNBRK 1000007
Wagon, bulk fuel Rolling stock used to move bulk fuel by rail. [Also often known as tank wagons.] Adapted OCBRH WGNFUL 1000008
Wagon, car transporter Rolling stock specifically designed to carry civilian or military cars by rail (for example EURORAIL). Adapted OCBRH WGNCAR 1000009
Wagon, cargo (enclosed) Rolling stock used to move boxed or palletised equipment/stores and closed by means of fixed doors either sliding or hinged. Adapted OCBRH WGNCRG 1000010
Wagon, cargo, sliding sides Rolling stock used to move boxed or palletised equipment/stores and closed by means of sliding curtained side panels. Adapted OCBRH WGNCSS 1000011
Wagon, cattle Rolling stock used to move livestock by rail. Adapted OCBRH WGNCTL 1000012
Wagon, flatbed Rolling stock used to move either large bulky cargoes, for example B vehicles, or large linear cargoes, for example logs/cut timber, replacement sections of track. Adapted OCBRH WGNFLB 1000013
Wagon, hopper Rolling stock used to move bulk loose material by rail and unloaded by gravity through bottom doors. [Mineral or foodstuffs e.g. grain are typical cargoes.] Adapted OCBRH WGNHPR 1000014
Wagon, liquid Rolling stock used to move bulk liquids by rail. Adapted OCBRH WGNLQD 1000016
Wagon, mineral Rolling stock used to move bulk mineral by rail with unloading facilities from either side. Adapted OCBRH WGNMNR 1000017
Wagon, open container Rolling stock used to move bulk loose items by rail. Adapted OCBRH WGNOPC 1000018
Wagon, passenger Rolling stock used for the transportation of passengers. Adapted OCBRH WGNPAS 1000019
Wagon, potable water Rolling stock used to move potable water by rail. Adapted OCBRH WGNWAT 1000020
Wagon, refrigerated Rolling stock used to move refrigerated cargoes by rail. Adapted OCBRH WGNRFG 1000021
Wagon, special purpose Rolling stock used to move specialised or outsized loads by rail, for example MBTs, other outsized loads. Adapted OCBRH WGNSPP 1000022
Wagon, track repair and maintenance Rolling stock used to repair (replace track) or maintain the railway track and track bed. Adapted OCBRH WGNRPR 1000023
Wagon, warflat Rolling stock used to move specific military loads, for example AIFVs (UK Warrior). Adapted OCBRH WGNWFL 1000024
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000025
Not otherwise specified The appropriate value is not in the set of specified values. ATCCIS NOS 1000026
