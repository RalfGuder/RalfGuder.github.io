Domain
Name: aircraft-type-main-purpose-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the main purpose of an AIRCRAFT-TYPE.
Definition Source: MIP-NDAG
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Air defense An aircraft that protects and defends an area, and/or a strike attack, and/or an installation, and/or naval forces. MIP-NDAG AIRDEF 1000005
Air superiority An aircraft that is deemed to obtain a degree of dominance in the air battle of one force over another in order to permit the conduct of operations by the former and its related land, sea and air forces at a given time and place without prohibitive interference by the opposite force. Adapted AAP-6 AIRSUP 1000006
Air to air refuelling An aircraft in which the objective is to enhance combat effectiveness by extending the range, payload or endurance of receiver aircraft. Adapted AJP 3.3 AAR 1000007
Airborne command post (C2) An aircraft that is the airborne element in which the unit or sub-unit commander is located or from which he operates. Adapted AAP-6 ACP 1000001
Airborne early warning An aircraft equipped with search and height finding radars and communications equipment designed to provide air surveillance. Adapted DCADM AEW 1000002
Airborne early warning and control An aircraft equipped with search and height finding radars and communications equipment designed to provide air surveillance and to control airborne weapons systems. DCADM AEWCON 1000003
Airborne relay An aircraft that employs a technique with radio relay for the purpose of increasing the range, flexibility or physical security of communication systems. Adapted AAP-6 ARELAY 1000004
Anti-armour An aircraft designed and primarily armed for use in the destruction of armoured targets. DCADM ANTARM 1000008
Anti-submarine warfare An aircraft employed in air operation conducted with the intention of denying the enemy the effective use of submarines. MTP-1 Vol 1 ASUW 1000009
Anti-submarine warfare (MPA) A maritime patrol aircraft conducting operations with the intention of denying the enemy the effective use of submarines. Adapted APP-6A ASMPA 1000011
Anti-submarine warfare (carrier based) An aircraft based on an aircraft carrier conducting operations with the intention of denying the enemy the effective use of submarines. Adapted APP-6A ASCB 1000010
Anti-surface (MPA) A maritime patrol aircraft conducting operations with the intention of denying the enemy the effective use of surface forces. Adapted APP-6A ASW 1000012
Armed assault An aircraft that enables a short, violent, but well ordered attack against a local objective such as a gun emplacement, a fort or a machine-gun nest. Adapted APP-6A ARMAS 1000013
Attack An aircraft specifically designed to employ various weapons to attack and destroy enemy targets. ADatP-3 FFIRN/FUD 1036/2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1549/1 - Def AAP-6 APR 2008 adapted ATTACK 1000014
Attack/strike An aircraft specifically designed to employ various weapons within a strike to attack and destroy enemy targets. ADatP-3 FFIRN/FUD 1036/2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1010/2 - Def AAP-6 APR 2008 adapted ATKSTR 1000015
Cargo airlift An aircraft used for the transport of cargo. MIP-NDAG CARGO 1000016
Command and control An aircraft designed and equipped to allow a commander to exercise authority and direction over assigned and attached forces in the accomplishment of the mission. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1874/3 adapted - Def JP 1-02 OCT 2007 adapted CMDCTL 1000017
Communications (C3I) An aircraft equipped with communication means such as data links or radio means for the purpose of consultation, command, control and intelligence. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1874/3 adapted - Def MIP-NDAG COM 1000018
Drone launch An aircraft used for the launch of drones. MIP-NDAG DRONL 1000019
Electronic counter measures An aircraft that operates in a division of electronic warfare involving actions taken to prevent or reduce an enemy's effective use of the electromagnetic spectrum through the use of electromagnetic energy. There are three subdivisions of electronic countermeasures: electronic jamming, electronic deception and electronic neutralization. Def AAP-6 adapted ELCCNM 1000020
Electronic counter measures (Jammer) An aircraft that operates in a division of electronic warfare involving actions taken to prevent or reduce an enemy's effective use of the electromagnetic spectrum through the use of electromagnetic energy by electronic jamming. Def adapted AAP-6 ECMJAM 1000021
Electronic warfare An aircraft designed for military action to exploit the electromagnetic spectrum encompassing: the search for, interception and identification of electromagnetic emissions, the employment of electromagnetic energy, including directed energy, to reduce or prevent hostile use of the electromagnetic spectrum, and actions to ensure its effective use by friendly forces. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1549/1 - Def AAP-6 APR 2008 adapted EW 1000022
Fighter-bomber An aircraft that combines the functions of a fighter and a bomber. OED FIGBOM 1000023
Fighter-interceptor An aircraft that combines the functions of a fighter and an interceptor. MIP-NDAG FIGINT 1000024
Ground attack An aircraft that performs ground attack. Adapted AAP-15 GDATK 1000025
Ground attack reconnaissance An aircraft that performs reconnaissance in order to gather information to prepare for ground attacks. MIP-NDAG GDATKR 1000026
Imagery intelligence gathering An aircraft designed and equipped with imaging sensors primarily for the purpose of gathering imagery for intelligence purposes. DCADM IMINGT 1000027
Liaison duties An aircraft that is devoted to liaise between different locations. MIP-NDAG LIAIS 1000028
Maintenance overhaul repair An aircraft that is equipped to perform the maintenance, overhaul or repair of other aircraft. MIP-NDAG MAINT 1000029
Maritime patrol An aircraft that flies over an area monitoring and where necessary destroying hostile aircraft as well as protecting friendly shipping in the vicinity of the objective area in amphibious operations. MIP-NDAG MPA 1000030
Maritime reconnaissance The role of the aircraft is to obtain, by radar detection and other sensory means information about the activities and resources of an enemy or potential enemy, or to secure data concerning the meteorological, hydrographic, or geographic characteristics of a particular area. MIP-NDAG MPAREC 1000031
Medical evacuation An aircraft designed and equipped to perform medical evacuation and treatment. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1549/1 - Def JP 1-02 OCT 2007 adapted MEDEVC 1000032
Meteorological An aircraft that gathers meteorological information such as humidity, pressure, and temperature characteristics of Earth's atmosphere. ADat-P3 BL12 1650/6, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1285/31 - Def OED adapted METBAL 1000033
Mine countermeasures An aircraft designed and equipped to perform mine countermeasures. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1285/31 adapted - Def JP 1-02 OCT 2007 adapted MINCM 1000035
Mine warfare An aircraft designed and equipped to perform mine warfare functions including mine laying and mine countermeasures. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1285/31 adapted - Def JP 1-02 OCT 2007 adapted MINWAR 1000034
Multi-purpose An aircraft that is capable of performing various missions. MIP-NDAG MULTIP 1000036
Multi-sensor An aircraft equipped with multi sensors that detect, and may indicate, and/or record objects and activities by means of energy or particles emitted, reflected, or modified by objects. Adapted AAP-6 MULTIS 1000037
Naval An aircraft that is capable of performing maritime missions. MIP-NDAG NAV 1000038
Naval attack An aircraft that is capable of performing naval attack. MIP-NDAG NAVATK 1000039
Passenger airlift An aircraft used for the transport of passengers. MIP-NDAG PSG 1000040
Patrol An aircraft flying over an objective area, over the force projected, over the critical area of a combat zone, or over an air-defense area for the purpose of intercepting and destroying hostile aircraft before they reach their target. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1549/1 - Def MIP-NDAG PATROL 1000041
Photo mapping An aircraft that produces a photograph or photomosaic upon which the grid lines, marginal data, contours, place names, boundaries, and other data may be added. MIP-NDAG PHOTO 1000042
Radio/radar calibration An aircraft that flies a specific route to calibrate radars and radios. MIP-NDAG RCALIB 1000043
Reconnaissance An aircraft intended to obtain, by visual observation or other detection methods, information about the activities and resources of an enemy or potential enemy, or to secure data concerning the meteorological, hydrographic, or geographic characteristics of a particular area. ADatP-3 FFIRN/FUD 1036/2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1549/1 - Def AAP-6 APR 2008 adapted RECCE 1000044
Reconnaissance, ECM An aircraft intended to obtain by detection means information about the ECM activities and resources of an enemy or potential enemy in a particular area. MIP-NDAG RECECM 1000045
Reconnaissance, photographic An aircraft intended to obtain, by photographic means information about the activities and resources of an enemy or potential enemy, or to secure data concerning the meteorological, hydrographic, or geographic characteristics of a particular area. ADatP-3 FFIRN/FUD 1036/2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1549/1 adapted - Def AAP-6 APR 2008 adapted RECPHO 1000046
Reconnaissance, radar The role of the aircraft is to obtain, by radar detection methods information about the activities and resources of an enemy or potential enemy, or to secure data concerning the meteorological, hydrographic, or geographic characteristics of a particular area. ADatP-3 FFIRN/FUD 1036/2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1549/1 adapted - Def AAP-6 APR 2008 adapted RECRAD 1000047
Reconnaissance, visual The role of the aircraft is to obtain, visual information about the activities and resources of an enemy or potential enemy, or to secure data concerning the meteorological, hydrographic, or geographic characteristics of a particular area. MIP-NDAG RECVIS 1000048
Scout An aircraft collecting intelligence without engaging enemy forces in order to participate in the close in security of a force. ADatP-3 BL12 1549/2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1549/1 - Def MIP-NDAG SCOUT 1000049
Search and rescue An aircraft designed and equipped to look for and bring back any lost, incapacitated or captured person or group of persons. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1010/4 - Def OED adapted SAR 1000050
Search and rescue (Combat) An aircraft designed and equipped to look for and bring back any lost, incapacitated or captured person or group of persons in a combat area. MIP-NDAG CSAR 1000051
Signals intelligence gathering An aircraft designed and equipped primarily for the purpose of intercepting and gathering electronic and communications transmissions for intelligence purposes. DCADM SINGA 1000052
Special operations forces An aircraft used for the transport of members of special operation forces. MIP-NDAG SOF 1000053
Special purpose An aircraft designed for a singular purpose. ADatP-3 BL12 1549/2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1549/1 - Def MIP-NDAG SPCPRP 1000054
Storage An aircraft under care and maintenance. MIP-NDAG STOR 1000055
Tanker An aircraft used for carrying fuel in bulk, esp. for the aerial refuelling of other aircraft. OED TANKER 1000056
Target/relay, reconnaissance An aircraft capable of obtaining information about the activities and resources of a target. MIP-NDAG TGTREL 1000057
Tow target An aircraft capable of towing a target. MIP-NDAG TOWTGT 1000058
Utility Multi-purpose aircraft capable of carrying troops but may be used in a command and control, logistics, casualty evacuation or armed aircraft role. AAP-6 UTILTY 1000059
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000060
Not otherwise specified The appropriate value is not in the set of specified values. ATCCIS NOS 1000061
