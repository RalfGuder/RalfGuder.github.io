Domain
Name: action-task-meteorological-impact-code
Definition: The specific value that represents a subjective indication of the effect of weather conditions on a specific operation.
Definition Source: ADatP-3 BL 13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1188/1
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Acceptable The meteorological conditions will not affect the specific operation. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1188/1 - Def MIP-NDAG ACCPTB 1000001
Marginal The meteorological conditions will render the specific operation with a moderate risk of failure. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1188/1 - Def MIP-NDAG MARGNL 1000002
Unacceptable The meteorological conditions will render the specific operation with a high risk of failure. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1188/1 - Def MIP-NDAG UNACC 1000003
