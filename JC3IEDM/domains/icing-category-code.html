Domain
Name: icing-category-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the class of a particular ICING.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Clear icing Glossy, clear, or translucent ice formed by the relatively slow freezing of large supercooled droplets. The droplets spread out over the airframe surface before completely freezing. APP-6A OCT 98 CLRICE 1000001
Mixed icing A hard rough conglomerate of ice that can cause very rough accumulation and severe loss of lift. APP-6A OCT 98 MIXICE 1000003
Rime icing Rough, milky opaque ice formed by the instantaneous freezing of small supercooled droplets which trap air within the ice as they strike the aircraft. APP-6A OCT 98 RIMICE 1000002
