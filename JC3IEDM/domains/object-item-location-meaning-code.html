Domain
Name: object-item-location-meaning-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the meaning of the LOCATION geometry as it pertains to the OBJECT-ITEM.
Definition Source: MIP-NDAG
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Addressee's present position The current position of an addressee. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1612/1 - Def MIP-DMSWG ADRPRP 1000014
Association with command post The point representing the location of the ORGANISATION is the same as that of its command post. MIP Ops Group ASSCP 1000004
Centre front of force/convoy A position in the centre of the front line of a force or convoy when the convoy is not in circular formation. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1612/1 adapted - Def MIP-DMSWG CTRMNB 1000011
Centre of mass (automated derivation) A point representing the mean position of an OBJECT-ITEM. It is calculated by automated means. MIP Ops Group COM 1000005
Centre of mass (manual derivation) A point representing the mean position of an OBJECT-ITEM. It is derived by manual means. Adapted COD CEOFMA 1000001
Commander determined A point representing the position of an OBJECT-ITEM in accordance to criteria specified by competent authority. MIP Ops Group CMDDET 1000006
Disposition centre The central position of a maritime force disposition. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1612/1 adapted - Def MIP-DMSWG DSPCTR 1000008
Formation centre The central point of a maritime force formation. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1612/1 adapted - Def MIP-DMSWG FRMCTR 1000009
Line of bearing A specific LOCATION representing the direction from an observer to the specific OBJECT-ITEM. The direction is specified by means of FAN-AREA geometry using the observer's position as the vertex. Adapted AAP-6 LNBRNG 1000003
Originator's present location Originators current position. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1612/1 - Def MIP-DMSWG ORGPRL 1000012
Position of intended movement The pre-planned movement of a unit or force between designated points detailed by either time, position, course and speed. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1612/1 - Def MIP-DMSWG POSOIM 1000010
Shape A location describing the external geometry of an OBJECT-ITEM. Adapted COD SHAPE 1000002
Sounding A measured water depth or spot depth which has been reduced to chart datum. STANAG 7074 SOUND 1000007
Standard position estab by OTC A position designated by the OTC in which all positional reports are to be based upon. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1612/1 adapted - Def MIP-DMSWG STDPOS 1000013
