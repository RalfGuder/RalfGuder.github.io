Domain
Name: military-organisation-type-service-code
Definition: The specific value that represents a military, paramilitary, irregular force, force or group, capable of functioning as an offensive or defensive combat or support organisation.
Definition Source: ADatP-3 FFIRN 1107 'Armed Service'
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Air Force The MILITARY-ORGANISATION-TYPE in question belongs to the Air Force (includes reserves and mobilised air national guard). ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1107/1 - Def ATCCIS AIRFRC 1000001
Army The MILITARY-ORGANISATION-TYPE in question belongs to the Army (includes territorial army, reserves, and mobilised national guard). ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1107/1 - Def ATCCIS ARMY 1000002
Border guard A paramilitary MILITARY-ORGANISATION-TYPE whose primary task is to maintain the security of national borders. ATCCIS BRDRGD 1000003
Civil service The MILITARY-ORGANISATION-TYPE that is staffed solely by civilian personnel. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1107/2 - Def MIP-NDAG CVLSVC 1000016
Coast guard The MILITARY-ORGANISATION-TYPE that may be responsible for one or more of the following: coastal defence, protection of life and property at sea, and enforcement of customs, immigration, and navigation laws. Adapted OED COASTG 1000004
Combined The MILITARY-ORGANISATION-TYPE contains two or more forces or agencies of two or more allies. AAP-6(T) COMBND 1000005
Guerrilla The MILITARY-ORGANISATION-TYPE in question is an irregular military force. Adapted JCS Pub 1-02 GUERLL 1000006
Joint The MILITARY-ORGANISATION-TYPE contains elements of more than one Service from the same nation. AAP-6(T) JOINT 1000007
Local defence force A military MILITARY-ORGANISATION-TYPE whose primary task is defence of a specific region (before mobilisation, a national guard is a local defence force). ATCCIS LCLDFF 1000008
Local militia A civilian MILITARY-ORGANISATION-TYPE that has a defence and possibly also a police role (may include irregular civilian MILITARY-ORGANISATION-TYPEs). ATCCIS LCLMLT 1000009
Marines The MILITARY-ORGANISATION-TYPE in question belongs to the Marines (includes reserves). ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1107/1 adapted - Def ATCCIS MARINE 1000010
Navy The MILITARY-ORGANISATION-TYPE in question belongs to the Navy (includes reserves). ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1107/1 - Def ATCCIS NAVY 1000011
Paramilitary Forces or groups distinct from the regular armed forces of any country, but resembling them in organization, equipment, training, or mission. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1107/2 - Def JP 1-02 OCT 2007 PAR 1000017
Special force The MILITARY-ORGANISATION-TYPE in question is trained and equipped for special purposes. Adapted USMTF SPFRC 1000012
Territorial force The MILITARY-ORGANISATION-TYPE of a nation's armed forces that is responsible for regional defence. Adapted USMTF TERFRC 1000013
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000014
Not otherwise specified The appropriate value is not in the set of specified values. ATCCIS NOS 1000015
