Domain
Name: consumable-materiel-type-category-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the class of CONSUMABLE-MATERIEL-TYPE.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
AMMUNITION-TYPE A CONSUMABLE-MATERIEL-TYPE that is a complete device charged with explosives, propellants, pyrotechnics, initiating composition, or nuclear, biological, or chemical material for use in military operations. AAP-6(2008) adapted AMMO 1000023
BIOLOGICAL-MATERIEL-TYPE A CONSUMABLE-MATERIEL-TYPE that is either a microorganism that causes disease in man, plants, or animals or causes the deterioration of materiel; or a toxin, produced by an animal, plant, or microorganism, which may kill, seriously injure, or incapacitate personnel through its physiological effects. AEP/ATP-45(B) BIOMAT 1000013
CHEMICAL-MATERIEL-TYPE A CONSUMABLE-MATERIEL-TYPE that is a substance that is not produced by a living organism, and does not emit radiation but may kill, seriously injure, or incapacitate personnel through its physiological effects or cause the deterioration of materiel. AEP/ATP-45(B) CHMMAT 1000014
Construction materials Any material that may be used in construction. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1231/1 - Def OED adapted CON 1000003
Crops Cultivated agricultural plants, as grain, vegetables or fruit. Adapted OED CROPS 1000021
Drug A narcotic, hallucinogen, or stimulant, esp. one causing addiction. Def - COD DRUG 1000025
Exercise mine In naval mine warfare, a mine suitable for use in mine warfare exercises, fitted with visible or audible indicating devices to show where and when it would normally fire. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1138/6 adapted - Def AAP-6 APR 2008 MAMNEX 1000028
Flare A pyrotechnic munition producing a bright light for illumination or identification. ADatP-3 BL11 FFIRN/FUD 1285/36, ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1407/28 - Def OED adapted FLARE 1000026
Food Materiel to be used as nourishment in solid or liquid form. Def adapted OED FOO 1000004
Fuel A substance burned to create energy. APP-9 - Def adapted OED FUEL 1000017
Fuse A device that initiates an explosive train. AAP-6 FUSE 1000019
General supplies All disposable materiel and items used in the equipment, support and maintenance of military forces or civilians. Adapted AAP-6 GENSPL 1000016
Improvised explosive device A device placed or fabricated in an improvised manner incorporating destructive, lethal, noxious, pyrotechnic or incendiary chemicals and designed to destroy, incapacitate, harass or distract. AAP-6 IMEXDE 1000018
Map A piece of paper that is a graphic representation of a part or the whole of the earth. AAP-6(V) - Def MIP-NDAG MAP 1000027
Medical supply Supplies for the treatment of injuries, illness and disease, including drugs. ATCCIS MEDSPL 1000024
Money The official currency, coins and negotiable paper notes issued by a government. Adapted OED MONEY 1000022
POL Petroleum, Oil, and Lubricant - A broad term that includes all petroleum and associated products used by the armed forces. AAP-6(T) (POL) POL 1000007
Personal equipment Equipment issued to or carried by an individual. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1231/1 - Def OED adapted PEREQU 1000012
RADIOACTIVE-MATERIEL-TYPE A CONSUMABLE-MATERIEL-TYPE that is a substance which spontaneously emits radiation, and which may kill, seriously injure, or incapacitate personnel through its physiological effects or causes the deterioration of materiel. AEP/ATP-45(B) RADMAT 1000015
Spare parts A generic term covering materiel used as replacement parts. Adapted UK JSP 101 SPRPRT 1000020
Water A clear, colourless, nearly odourless and tasteless liquid. OED WAT 1000011

Not otherwise specified The appropriate value is not in the set of specified values. ATCCIS NOS 1000029
