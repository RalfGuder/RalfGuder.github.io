Domain
Name: airfield-type-use-category-code
Definition: The specific value indicating an airport's main use.
Definition Source: Adapted DAFIF
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Civil Civil airports controlled and operated by civil authorities primarily used by civil aircrafts although military aircrafts may have landing privileges and/or contract rights. Adapted DAFIF A 1000001
Joint Joint (civil and military) airports jointly controlled, used or operated by both civil and military agencies. Adapted DAFIF B 1000002
Limited Airfields having permanent type surface runways with less than the minimum facilities required for civil, joint or military airports. Adapted DAFIF D 1000003
Military Military airports controlled and operated by military authorities primarily used by military aircrafts although civil aircrafts may have landing privileges and/or contract rights. Adapted DAFIF C 1000004
