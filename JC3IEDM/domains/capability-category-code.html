Domain
Name: capability-category-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the general class of a CAPABILITY.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
ENGINEERING-CAPABILITY A CAPABILITY, required for planning, of those ORGANISATIONs and PERSONs or ORGANISATION-TYPEs and PERSON-TYPEs that are deemed as having the ability to perform construction or destruction activities. MIP-NDAG ENGI 1000002
FIRE-CAPABILITY A CAPABILITY, required for planning, of those FACILITYs, MATERIELs, ORGANISATIONs and PERSONs, or FACILITY-TYPEs, EQUIPMENT-TYPEs, ORGANISATION-TYPEs and PERSON-TYPEs that are deemed as having the ability to discharge or launch a projectile or missile. MIP-NDAG FIRE 1000003
HANDLING-CAPABILITY A CAPABILITY, required for planning, of those FACILITYs and MATERIELs, or FACILITY-TYPEs and EQUIPMENT-TYPEs that are deemed as having the ability to move materiels (raw materials, scrap, semi-finished, and finished) to, through, and from productive processes; in warehouses and storage; and in receiving and shipping areas. Adapted AAP-6 HNDLNG 1000021
MAINTENANCE-CAPABILITY A CAPABILITY, required for planning, of those FACILITYs, MATERIELs, ORGANISATIONs and PERSONs or FACILITY-TYPEs, EQUIPMENT-TYPEs, ORGANISATION-TYPEs, and PERSON-TYPEs that are deemed as having the ability to provide a range of activities required to restore or maintain operational usage. ACT MAIN 1000005
MOBILITY-CAPABILITY A CAPABILITY, required for planning, of those FACILITYs, MATERIELs, ORGANISATIONs and PERSONs or FACILITY-TYPEs, EQUIPMENT-TYPEs, ORGANISATION-TYPEs, and PERSON-TYPEs that are deemed as having the nominal ability to move in space, air, on water, under water, or over a specific type of terrain. MIP-NDAG MOBL 1000014
OPERATIONAL-CAPABILITY A CAPABILITY, required for planning, of those objects and types of objects that are deemed as having the ability, the training and the equipment to perform an operation. MIP-NDAG OPERAT 1000024
STORAGE-CAPABILITY A CAPABILITY, required for planning, of those FACILITYs and MATERIELs or EQUIPMENT-TYPEs and FACILITY-TYPEs that are deemed as having the ability to hold a specific OBJECT-TYPE. MIP-NDAG STOR 1000008
SUPPORT-CAPABILITY A CAPABILITY, required for planning, of those FACILITYs, MATERIELs and ORGANISATIONs or FACILITY-TYPEs, EQUIPMENT-TYPEs and ORGANISATION-TYPEs that are deemed as having the ability to provide supplies or services. ATCCIS SUPPRT 1000022
SURVEILLANCE-CAPABILITY A CAPABILITY, required for planning, of those FACILITYs, MATERIELs, ORGANISATIONs and PERSONs or FACILITY-TYPEs, EQUIPMENT-TYPEs, ORGANISATION-TYPEs and PERSON-TYPEs that are deemed as having the nominal ability to observe aerospace, surface or subsurface areas, places, persons, or things, by visual, aural, electronic, photographic or other means. ATCCIS SURV 1000009
TRANSMISSION-CAPABILITY A CAPABILITY, required for planning, of those MATERIELs or MATERIEL-TYPEs that are deemed as having the ability to generate, receive or affect signals in the electromagnetic spectrum. MIP-NDAG TRANSM 1000023
