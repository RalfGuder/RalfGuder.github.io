Domain
Name: quay-storage-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the class of storage facilities available at a specific QUAY.
Definition Source: MIP
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
CBRN The equipment and resources to load/unload, keep temporarily and to manage in a correct way military chemical, biological, radiological, or nuclear materiel and associated equipment. CBRN COI CBRN 1000005
Grain silo A tower or towers for the storage of grain, typically accompanied by loading/offloading facilities for grain. Adapted OED GRNSIL 1000001
Hazardous cargo The equipment and resources to securely store, in transit, any cargo that contains a substance that is harmful to humans or living organisms and poses a physical hazard, health hazard or environmental hazard. This includes substances that are toxic, explosive, corrosive, nuclear, ignitable or chemically reactive. Adapted, various sources HAZCAR 1000002
Liquid storage The equipment and resources to hold and store a liquid in transit, and normally has inloading and discharging facilities. Adapted OED LQDSTR 1000003
Munitions and explosives The equipment and resources to load/unload, keep temporarily and to manage in a correct way military weapons, ammunition, explosives and associated equipment. Adapted OED MNTEXP 1000004
Open area A flat open area, prepared (tarmac, concrete) or natural, on which cargo can be loaded/offloaded from vessels and stored whilst in transit. Adapted OED OPENAR 1000006
Refrigerated A building, usually insulated, with a cooled or chilled inside environment, normally used for keeping in transit anything that is perishable, such as, foodstuffs. Adapted OED RFRGTD 1000007
Vehicle The equipment and resources for storing road vehicles in transit. This may be an open area or a multi storey building having several storeys or floors or both, accessible to vehicles by means of ramps. Adapted OED VEHCLE 1000008
Warehouse A large structure used for the storage of goods or material in transit. Adapted OED WARHSE 1000009
Not otherwise specified The appropriate value is not in the set of specified values. ATCCIS NOS 1000010
