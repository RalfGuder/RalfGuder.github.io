Domain
Name: affiliation-functional-group-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the category of functional group.
Definition Source: MIP
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Criminal Affiliation is directed to a group that is organised for the conduct of criminal activity. OPSITREP IA A011 Draft FFIRN 1177 - Def MIP-DMSWG CRIMIN 1000001
Exercise Affiliation is directed to a group that exists solely for the purpose of practice, rehearsal or training in the conduct of operations. MIP-NDAG EXER 1000006
Financial Affiliation is directed to a group that provides financial support. OPSITREP IA A011 Draft FFIRN 1177 - Def MIP-DMSWG FINANC 1000007
Multinational Affiliation is directed to a group whose charter is based on a treaty signed by multiple nations. MIP MULTIN 1000002
Political Affiliation is directed to a group that is involved within a political area of activity and concerned with politics or has ties to a political party. OPSITREP IA A011 Draft FFIRN 1177 - Def MIP-DMSWG POLTCL 1000008
Terrorist Affiliation is directed to a group that is organised for the conduct of terrorist activity. MIP TERRST 1000003
Tribal Affiliation is directed to a unit of socio-political organization consisting of a number of families, clans, or other groups who share a common ancestry and culture. OPSITREP IA A011 Draft FFIRN 1177 - Def MIP-DMSWG TRIBAL 1000009
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000004
Not otherwise specified The appropriate value is not in the set of specified values. ATCCIS NOS 1000005
