Domain
Name: road-shoulder-width-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the average horizontal distance measured from side to side and perpendicular to the central axis of a specific hard shoulder (lane/area beside a highway for broken-down or not running vehicles).
Definition Source: Adapted AIntP-3 and NTDI (NATO Target Data Inventory)
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
1 to 2m The specific hard shoulder is between 1 and 2 metres. Adapted NTDI 2 1000002
Over 2m The specific hard shoulder is over 2 metres. Adapted NTDI 1 1000001
Under 1m The specific hard shoulder is under 1 metre. Adapted NTDI 3 1000003
Usage:
Entity Attribute Opt
