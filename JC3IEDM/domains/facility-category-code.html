Domain
Name: facility-category-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the class of FACILITY.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
AIRFIELD A FACILITY that is an area prepared for the accommodation (including any buildings, installations, or equipment) landing and take off of aircraft. ATCCIS AIRFLD 1000006
ANCHORAGE A FACILITY that is a place where vessels anchor. MIP-NDAG ANCHOR 1000011
APRON A FACILITY that is an area intended for parking, loading, unloading and/or servicing. Adapted AAP-6 and WPI APRON 1000020
BASIN A FACILITY that is an open area of water, usually artificial and enclosed by dock gates lined with wharves, warehouses and berths to enable vessels to load and unload. MIP-NDAG BASIN 1000012
BERTH A FACILITY that is a space or length in the water at a harbour allocated to or reserved for a vessel to dock and moor for loading or unloading. MIP-NDAG BERTH 1000013
BRIDGE A FACILITY that is a structure (including overpass and viaduct), fixed or moveable, spanning and/or providing passage over an object. ATCCIS BRIDGE 1000002
DRY-DOCK A FACILITY that provides an enclosure for maintenance, building or repairing ships, from which water can be pumped out. MIP-NDAG DRYDCK 1000014
HARBOUR A FACILITY that is a restricted body of water, an anchorage, or other limited coastal water area and its water approaches from which and in which shipping operations are projected or supported. APP-6A OCT 98 - Def JCS Pub 1-02 HARBOR 1000009
JETTY A FACILITY that is a platform that may be fixed or floating extending from a shore, normally attached to a wharf or the shore, and which allows access to a vessel lying alongside, used to secure, protect and provide landing and docking for vessels. MIP-NDAG JETTY 1000015
MILITARY-OBSTACLE A FACILITY designed to stop, impede, or divert movement of amphibious or ground forces. Adapted USMTF MILOBS 1000008
NETWORK A FACILITY that provides bearer services for communication and information services and is composed of one or more links and nodes. ATCCIS NETWRK 1000007
QUAY A FACILITY that is a solidly constructed platform, usually parallel to the shoreline of navigable water, alongside which a vessel can be docked or berthed and, on which, the vessel can be accessed and cargo can be loaded or unloaded on one side of the vessel only. MIP-NDAG QUAY 1000016
RAILWAY A FACILITY that is a track or set of tracks made of steel rails along which trains run. Adapted OED RALWAY 1000018
ROAD A FACILITY that is a path or way with a specially prepared surface. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN 1704 adapted - Def MIP-NDAG ROAD 1000019
RUNWAY A FACILITY that is a specifically prepared surface along which aircraft take off and land. Adapted DOD Dictionary of Military and Associated Terms RUNWAY 1000010
SLIPWAY A FACILITY that provides a sloping surface or inclined structure leading down to the water. MIP-NDAG SLPWAY 1000017
Not otherwise specified The appropriate value is not in the set of specified values. ATCCIS NOS 1000003
