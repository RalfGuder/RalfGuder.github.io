Domain
Name: materiel-status-operational-status-mode-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the firepower or mobility or communications degradation of a specific MATERIEL.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Communications only An indication that the associated degraded operational status only applies to the communications of the specified item of MATERIEL. MIP CO 1000005
Firepower and communications An indication that the associated degraded operational status applies to both the firepower and the communications of the specified item of MATERIEL. ATCCIS FC 1000006
Firepower only An indication that the associated degraded operational status only applies to the firepower of the specified item of MATERIEL. ATCCIS FO 1000001
Firepower, mobility and communications An indication that the associated degraded operational status applies to the firepower, the mobility and the communications of the specified item of MATERIEL. ATCCIS FMC 1000007
Mobility and communications An indication that the associated degraded operational status applies to both the mobility and the communications of the specified item of MATERIEL. ATCCIS MC 1000008
Mobility and firepower An indication that the associated degraded operational status applies to both the mobility and the firepower of the specified item of MATERIEL. ATCCIS MF 1000002
Mobility only An indication that the associated degraded operational status only applies to the mobility of the specified item of MATERIEL. ATCCIS MO 1000003
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000004
Usage:
Entity Attribute Opt
