Domain
Name: mine-status-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the status of a mine.
Definition Source: ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1609/1 adapted and UK MWTU
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Activated mine The specified mine has been activated through either influence or control signal. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1609/1 - Def MWTU ACTVED 1000001
Classified mine The specified mine has been located and has been evaluated by a competent authority. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1609/1 - Def ATP-6(c) Vol 1 adapted CLSSFD 1000002
Countermined mine The specified mine has been exploded by the shock of a nearby explosion of another mine or independent explosive charge. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1609/1 - Def ATP-6(c) Vol 1 adapted CNTRMN 1000003
Identified mine The specified mine has been located, has been processed by a competent authority and its exact nature is known. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1609/1 - Def ATP-6(c) Vol 1 adapted IDNTMN 1000004
Marked mine The specified mine is located and its presence is indicated by some form of physical identification device. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1609/1 - Def MIP-NDAG MRKDMN 1000005
Mine left in place The specified mine is located and remains in its original location. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1609/1 - Def MIP-NDAG MNLFPL 1000006
Neutralized mine The specified mine is rendered, by external means, permanently incapable of firing, although it may remain dangerous to handle. The mine case may remain virtually intact. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1609/1 adapted - Def ATP-6(c) Vol 1 adapted NEUTRL 1000007
Recovered mine The specified mine is salvaged as nearly intact as possible to permit further investigation for intelligence and/or evaluation purposes. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1609/1 - Def ATP-6(c) Vol 1 adapted RECVRD 1000008
Removed mine The specified mine is taken out of an area where its detonation would be unacceptable. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1609/1 - Def ATP-6(c) Vol 1 adapted REMOVD 1000009
Rendered safe mine The specified mine is made safe by the application of special explosive ordnance disposal methods and tools for the interruption of function or separation of essential components of unexploded ordnance to prevent an unacceptable detonation. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1609/1 - Def ATP-6(c) Vol 1 adapted RNDERD 1000010
Sunk mine The specified mine has lost its buoyancy. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1609/1 - Def MWTU SUNKMN 1000011
