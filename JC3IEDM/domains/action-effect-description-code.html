Domain
Name: action-effect-description-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the type of outcome of a specific ACTION that is being estimated or recorded.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Burning The ACTION objective is alight. Adapted OED BURN 1000001
Captured The ACTION objective has been captured or acquired and is available for use or interrogation/possession has been taken of an area, normally by force. Adapted OED CAPTRD 1000002
Consumed The consumption of specified consumables or CONSUMABLE-MATERIEL-TYPEs. ATCCIS CONS 1000003
Destroyed (K-kill) Cannot function as intended nor be repaired or restored to an operational status. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUDs 1088/4/8/9 - Def MIP-NDAG DSTRYK 1000004
Firepower kill (F-kill) The destruction of a vehicle (or system's) primary weapon system. ATCCIS FKIL 1000005
Forced to flee The setting to flight of a PERSON or PERSON-TYPEs from their present location. Adapted OED FLIG 1000015
Identified The ACTION objective has been either identified (i.e., classified as friend/foe/neutral) or recognised (i.e., the unit designation of the ORGANISATION or object is known). Adapted AAP-6 IDNT 1000006
Illuminated The temporary supply of IR or white-light illumination (usually in coordination with direct or indirect fire). Adapted OED ILLUMN 1000007
Intact but unrecoverable An equipment lost in an area unsuitable for recovery due to political, military or geographic/environmental considerations. ADat-P3 BL12, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1374/1 - Def ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1374/1 adapted INTREC 1000024
Killed A casualty who is killed outright or dies as a result of wounds, disease or other injuries. Adapted AAP-6 KILL 1000017
Light damage The subjective categorisation of the physical effect of an ACTION on buildings, infrastructure and/or equipment by a reporting ORGANISATION. ATCCIS LDAM 1000008
Light resistance The ACTION-OBJECTIVE reacted against the ACTION using light physical violence. ATCCIS LGTRST 1000020
Lost The object is no longer available for military operations. MIP-NDAG LOST 1000025
Mobility kill (M-kill) The rendering of a vehicle as being temporarily or permanently incapable of tactical movement. ATCCIS MKIL 1000009
Moderate damage The subjective categorisation of the physical effect of an ACTION on buildings, infrastructure and/or equipment by a reporting ORGANISATION. ATCCIS MODDAM 1000010
Neutralized The rendering of the ACTION objective temporarily ineffective by the infliction of significant casualties (norm for indirect fire is 10 percent). UK SOHB adapted NUTRLD 1000011
No resistance The ACTION-OBJECTIVE did not react against the ACTION. ATCCIS NORSTN 1000021
Non-battle casualty A person who is not a battle casualty, but who is lost to his organisation by reason of disease or injury, including persons dying from disease or injury, or by reason of being missing where the absence does not appear to be voluntary or due to enemy action or to being interned. AAP-6 NBCAS 1000016
Severe damage The subjective categorisation of the physical effect of an ACTION on buildings, infrastructure and/or equipment by a reporting ORGANISATION. ATCCIS SDAM 1000012
Suppressed Reduction of the effectiveness of the ACTION objective for a specific period or purpose (normally to prevent an enemy from interfering with friendly ACTIONs); this effect may be entirely transitory and cause no casualties. Adapted UK SOHB SUPRSD 1000013
Verbal protest The ACTION-OBJECTIVE reacted against the ACTION using verbal abuse. ATCCIS VRBPRT 1000022
Violent resistance The ACTION-OBJECTIVE reacted against the ACTION using heavy physical violence. ATCCIS VLNRST 1000023
Wounded A casualty other than "killed" who has incurred an injury due to an external agent or cause. Adapted AAP-6 WNDD 1000014
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000018
Not otherwise specified The appropriate value is not in the set of specified values. ATCCIS NOS 1000019
