Domain
Name: materiel-status-operational-status-qualifier-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the qualification of the operational status of a specific MATERIEL.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Cleared The piece of materiel is weapon and explosive free. ATCCIS CLEARD 1000011
Denied Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a MATERIEL is unavailable through means such as removal, contamination or erection of obstructions. ATCCIS adapted AAP-6(T) DENIED 1000001
Destroyed Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a MATERIEL is not, and not expected ever to be, capable of performing the missions or functions for which it is designed. ATCCIS adapted AAP-6(T) (Operational Readiness) DSTRYD 1000002
Disassembled Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that the MATERIEL is taken apart in a way that it can be reassembled. ATCCIS DISASM 1000012
Heavily damaged Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation to be heavily damaged. ATCCIS HVYDAM 1000003
Immobilised Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a specific materiel or its component is incapable of movement in its current condition. ATCCIS IMMBLS 1000013
In maintenance Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a specific materiel is under some kind of service. ATCCIS INMNT 1000014
Intact but unrecoverable Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation to be in an area unsuitable for recovery due to political, military, or geographic/environmental considerations. ADatP-3 BL12, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1374/1 - Def OED adapted INTREC 1000015
Lacking vital resources Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a MATERIEL is deficient or lacking of some mission-critical resources (e.g., fuel, ammunition). ATCCIS LVR 1000004
Lightly damaged Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation to be only lightly damaged. ATCCIS LGTDAM 1000005
Lost Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a specific materiel is missing under unknown circumstances. ATCCIS LST 1000006
Moderately damaged Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation to be moderately damaged. ATCCIS MODDAM 1000007
Mothballed Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a specific materiel has been preserved to some degree. ATCCIS MTHBLD 1000009
Scrapped Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a specific materiel is dismantled or taken apart into pieces to store or destroy. ATCCIS SCRPPD 1000010
Sterilized Subjectively judged by the reporting organisation that a specific materiel has been made incapable of functioning productively or effectively. ADatP-3 BL12.2, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1205/1 - Def ATP-6 (c) Vol 1 adapted STERLZ 1000016
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000008
