Name: chemical-biological-event-spill-size-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the mass or the volume of a materiel spilled in a CHEMICAL-BIOLOGICAL-EVENT that is a release other than attack (ROTA).
Definition Source: CBRN COI
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Extra large Greater than 1500 litres in volume or 1500 kilograms in mass. ATP-45 XLG 1000001
Large Equal to or greater than 208 litres and less than or equal to 1500 litres in volume or equal to or greater than 200 kilograms and less than or equal to 1500 kilograms in mass. ATP-45 LRG 1000002
Small Less than 208 litres in volume or 200 kilograms in mass. ATP-45 SML 1000003
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000004
