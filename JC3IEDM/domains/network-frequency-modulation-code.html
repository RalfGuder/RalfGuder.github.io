Domain
Name: network-frequency-modulation-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the modulation of a specific NETWORK.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Double side band The modulation of a wave by varying its amplitude across the whole channel. ATCCIS DBSBND 1000001
Frequency modulation The modulation of a radio or other wave by variation of its frequency. ATCCIS FRQMOD 1000002
Frequency shift keying The modulation of a radio or other wave by shifting central frequency of the signal. ATCCIS FRQSHF 1000003
Lower side band The modulation of a wave by varying its amplitude with modulation on the lower side band of the channel. ATCCIS LWSBND 1000004
Phase shift keying The modulation of a radio or other wave by shifting phase of the signal. ATCCIS PHSHKY 1000005
Upper side band The modulation of a wave by varying its amplitude with modulation on the upper side band of the channel. ATCCIS UPSBND 1000006
