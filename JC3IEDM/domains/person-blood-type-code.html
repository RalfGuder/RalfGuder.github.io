Domain
Name: person-blood-type-code
Definition: A code which represents the specific blood type of a PERSON.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
A Rh Negative The circulating red blood cells with 'A' antigen and the absence of the 'Rh' antigen. Adapted DoD DDDS AMNUS 1000002
A Rh Positive The circulating red blood cells with 'A' antigen and the presence of the 'Rh' antigen. Adapted DoD DDDS APLUS 1000001
AB Rh Negative The circulating red blood cells with 'A' and 'B' antigen and the absence of the 'Rh' antigen. Adapted DoD DDDS ABMNUS 1000006
AB Rh Positive The circulating red blood cells with 'A' and 'B' antigen and the presence of the 'Rh' antigen. Adapted DoD DDDS ABPLUS 1000005
B Rh Negative The circulating red blood cells with 'B' antigen and the absence of the 'Rh' antigen. Adapted DoD DDDS BMNUS 1000004
B Rh Positive The circulating red blood cells with 'B' antigen and the presence of the 'Rh' antigen. Adapted DoD DDDS BPLUS 1000003
O Rh Negative The circulating red blood cells that lack 'A' and 'B' antigen and the absence of the 'Rh' antigen. Adapted DoD DDDS OMNUS 1000008
O Rh Positive The circulating red blood cells that lack 'A' and 'B' antigen and the presence of the 'Rh' antigen. Adapted DoD DDDS OPLUS 1000007
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000010
