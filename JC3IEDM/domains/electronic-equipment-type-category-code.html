Domain
Name: electronic-equipment-type-category-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the class of ELECTRONIC-EQUIPMENT-TYPE.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
C3I Equipment specifically designed to be used for Command, Control, Communications and Intelligence (C3I) support. Adapted AAP-6(V) C3I 1000001
Communication A group of interrelated communications equipment utilising the electromagnetic spectrum for the transmission and/or receiving of speech and data information. Adapted OED COM 1000002
Data-processing Equipment for storing, sharing and manipulation of data. AIntP-3 - Def adapted OED DPE 1000003
Electronic warfare An equipment used for military action involving the use of electromagnetic energy to determine, exploit, reduce, or prevent hostile use of the electromagnetic spectrum and action to retain its effective use by friendly forces. AAP-6 EW 1000004
Fire control A group of interrelated fire control equipments and/or instruments designed for use with a weapon or group of weapons. Adapted AAP-6(V) FRC 1000005
Instrument landing system An ELECTRONIC-EQUIPMENT-TYPE that provides aircraft with horizontal and vertical guidance just before landing and during landing, and at certain fixed points, indicates the distance to the reference point of landing. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1650/6 - Def AComP-01 ILS 1000012
Navigational aid Any electronic device which provides point-to-point guidance information or position data. OED NAV 1000006
Public address system A device capable of delivering voice messages. ATCCIS - Def adapted OED PAS 1000007
Radar Radio equipment based on emission of radio waves towards an object and analysis of the waves returned by that object or emitted in response to excitation by the received waves. AComP-01 A Oct. 2000 RADAR 1000011
Sensor A device that detects or measures a physical property and records, indicates, or otherwise responds to it. OED SEN 1000008
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000009
Not otherwise specified The appropriate value is not in the set of specified values. ATCCIS NOS 1000010
