Domain
Name: atmosphere-temperature-gradient-code
Definition: The specific value that represents heat change with respect to the ground and 100 m in elevation in a certain area. Acts as an indication of vertical air movement between the ground and higher elevations.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Neutral A one degree Kelvin, or less, temperature gradient between the ground and 100 metres above ground level. ATCCIS - adapted ATP-45(B) NEUTRL 1000007
Stable More than one degree Kelvin positive temperature gradient between the ground and 100 metres above ground level. (colder lower layers) ATCCIS - adapted ATP-45(B) STABLE 1000005
Unstable More than one degree Kelvin negative temperature gradient between the ground and 100 metres above ground level. (hotter lower layers) ATCCIS - adapted ATP-45(B) UNSTAB 1000006
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000003
