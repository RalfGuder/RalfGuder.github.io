Domain
Name: liquid-surface-status-surface-condition-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the physical status of a liquid surface area.
Definition Source: MIP-NDAG
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Drained A characterisation of an area temporarily without any liquid present. MIP-NDAG DRAIND 1000001
Ice A characterisation of an area covered with a layer or mass of frozen water. MIP-NDAG ICE 1000002
Liquid A characterisation of an area covered with liquid that is not frozen. MIP-NDAG LIQUID 1000003
Mixed A characterisation of an area covered with a mix of ice and water. MIP-NDAG MIXED 1000004
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable. ATCCIS NKN 1000005
Usage:
Entity Attribute Opt
LIQUID-SURFACE-STATUS liquid-surface-status-surface-condition-code OP
