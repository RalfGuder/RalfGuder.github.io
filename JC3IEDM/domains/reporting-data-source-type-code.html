Domain
Name: reporting-data-source-type-code
Definition: The specific value that identifies the source type from which intelligence information is obtained and which is referred to by a specific REPORTING-DATA.
Definition Source: ATCCIS
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Air observer The intelligence information is derived from an individual whose primary mission is to observe or take photographs from an aircraft. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1243/3 - Def MIP-NDAG AOBSR 1000025
Air reconnaissance The intelligence information is gathered either by visual observation from the air or through the use of airborne sensors. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1243/3 - Def AAP-6 APR 2008 adapted AIRREC 1000001
Airborne infrared The intelligence information is derived from airborne infrared systems. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def AAP-6 (2008) adapted AIRIFR 1000024
Artillery observation The intelligence information is derived from artillery unit surveillance. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def ATCCIS ARTOBS 1000002
Captured document The intelligence information is derived from documentation seized from the enemy. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def ATCCIS CAPDOC 1000003
Captured material The intelligence information is derived from equipment or supplies seized from the enemy. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def ATCCIS CAPMAT 1000004
Communications intelligence The intelligence information is derived from electromagnetic communications and communications systems by other than intended recipients or users. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def AAP-6 (2008) adapted COMINT 1000005
Contact The discrete airborne, surface or subsurface intelligence information is collected from electronic, acoustic, and/or visual sensors. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def AAP-6 (2008) adapted CONTAC 1000006
Defector The intelligence information is collected from a person who repudiates his or her country when beyond its jurisdiction or control. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def AAP-6 (2008) adapted DEFECT 1000007
Electronic intelligence The intelligence information is derived from electromagnetic non-communications transmissions by other than intended recipients or users. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def AAP-6 (2008) adapted ELINT 1000008
Eyeball observation The intelligence information is derived from a human observation without any device. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def ATCCIS EYOBSN 1000009
Flash ranging The intelligence information is derived from flash ranging devices. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1243/3 - Def MIP-NDAG FLRNG 1000026
Forward observer The intelligence information is derived from an observer with forward troops trained to call for and adjust supporting fire and pass battlefield information. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1243/3 - Def MIP-NDAG FO 1000027
Forward observer without laser The intelligence information is derived from an observer with forward troops without laser systems. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1243/3 - Def MIP-NDAG FOWOL 1000028
Ground reconnaissance The intelligence information is derived from ground reconnaissance tasks. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def ATCCIS GRDREC 1000010
Human intelligence The intelligence information is collected and provided by human sources. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def AAP-6 (2008) adapted HUMINT 1000011
Inflight The intelligence information is reported as mission results by aircrews while in flight. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def AAP-6 (2008) adapted INFLIT 1000012
Long range reconnaissance patrol The intelligence information is derived from ground reconnaissance behind the FLOT. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def ATCCIS LRRP 1000013
Observer, not artillery The intelligence information is derived from non-artillery observation. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1243/3 - Def MIP-NDAG OBSR 1000029
Photo interpretation The intelligence information is derived from extraction from photographs or other recorded images. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1243/3 - Def MIP-NDAG PI 1000030
Prisoner of war The intelligence information is derived from captured forces. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def ATCCIS POW 1000015
Radar, counter battery The intelligence information is derived from counter battery radar systems. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1243/3 adapted - Def MIP-NDAG CBRR 1000031
Radar, counter mortar The intelligence information is derived from counter mortar radar systems. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1243/3 adapted - Def MIP-NDAG CMRR 1000032
Radar, ground surveillance The intelligence information is derived from ground surveillance radar systems. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1243/3 adapted - Def MIP-NDAG GSRA 1000033
Radar, side looking The intelligence information is derived from an airborne radar, viewing at right angles to the axis of the vehicle, which produces a presentation of terrain or moving targets. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1243/3 - Def AAP-6 APR 2008 adapted SLAR 1000034
Refugee The intelligence information is derived from persons who, owing to religious persecution or political troubles, move within their own country (national refugees) or across international boundaries. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def AAP-6 (2008) adapted REFUGE 1000017
Remotely-piloted vehicle The intelligence information is derived from an unmanned vehicle capable of being controlled from a distant location through a communication link. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def AAP-6 (2008) adapted RPV 1000018
Satellite The intelligence information is derived from satellite surveillance or reconnaissance. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def ATCCIS SAT 1000019
Signal intelligence The intelligence information is collected either by communications intelligence or electronic intelligence when there is no requirement to differentiate between these two types of intelligence, or represents a fusion of information from the two. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def AAP-6 (2008) adapted SIGINT 1000020
Sound ranging The intelligence information is derived from sound ranging devices. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1243/3 - Def MIP-NDAG SORNG 1000035
Tactical air The intelligence information is derived from tactical air operations. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1243/3 - Def MIP-NDAG TACAIR 1000036
Target base The intelligence information is derived from target bases. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1243/3 - Def MIP-NDAG TGTB 1000037
Unattended ground sensor The intelligence information is derived from an unmanned ground sensor device. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def ATCCIS UGS 1000021
Unspecified sensitive source The source of the intelligence information is not specified. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def ATCCIS UNSPEC 1000022
Various sources The intelligence information is derived from different sources. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1310/1 - Def ATCCIS VARI 1000023
