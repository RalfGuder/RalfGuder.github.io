Domain
Name: anchorage-bottom-type-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the description of the ground under the water of a lake, river, ocean, or other body of water at a specific ANCHORAGE.
Definition Source: ADatP-3 BL 11.0 (FFIRN 1423)
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Boulder The surface consists of boulders larger than human head size. ADatP-3 BL11, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1423/4 adapted - Def MIP-DMSWG BOULDR 1000001
Clay The surface consists of clay. ADatP-3 BL11, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1423/4 - Def MIP-DMSWG CLAY 1000002
Cobbles The surface consists of cobbles up to human head size. ADatP-3 BL11, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1423/4 adapted - Def MIP-DMSWG COBBLS 1000003
Coral The surface consists of coral. ADatP-3 BL11, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1423/4 - Def MIP-DMSWG CORAL 1000004
Gravel The surface consists of gravel. ADatP-3 BL11, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1423/4 adapted - Def MIP-DMSWG GRAVEL 1000005
Limestone The surface consists of limestone. ADatP-3 BL11, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1423/4 - Def MIP-DMSWG LMNSTN 1000006
Mud The surface consists of mud. ADatP-3 BL11, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1423/4 - Def MIP-DMSWG MUD 1000007
Mud/smooth sand The surface consists of mud and smooth sand. Adapted ADatP-3 BL11 MUDSND 1000008
Pebbles The surface consists of pebbles up to clenched fist size. ADatP-3 BL11, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1423/4 adapted - Def MIP-DMSWG PEBBLS 1000009
Rock The surface is made of rock, not specified. ADatP-3 BL11, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1423/4 - Def MIP-DMSWG ROCK 1000010
Rough The surface of the bottom is uneven, with holes, bumps and folds up to 30cm and/or a lot of seaweed. ADatP-3 BL11, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1423/4 adapted - Def MIP-DMSWG ROUGH 1000011
Rough, coral/rock The surface of the bottom is uneven and contains outcrops of rock and or coral. Adapted ADatP-3 BL11 RGHCRL 1000012
Sand The surface consists of fine sand up to pinhead size. ADatP-3 BL11, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1423/4 adapted - Def MIP-DMSWG SAND 1000013
Sand, coarse The surface consists of sand between 0.5 and 2.0 mm in size. ADatP-3 BL11, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1423/4 adapted - Def MIP-DMSWG SANDCR 1000014
Sand, fine The surface consists of sand between 0.125 and 0.25mm in size. ADatP-3 BL11, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1423/4 adapted - Def MIP-DMSWG SANDFN 1000015
Sand, medium The surface consists of sand between 0.25 and 0.5mm in size. ADatP-3 BL11, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1423/4 adapted - Def MIP-DMSWG SANDMD 1000016
Sand, very coarse The surface consists of sand up to 72mm in size. ADatP-3 BL11, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1423/4 adapted - Def MIP-DMSWG SANDVC 1000017
Sand, very fine The surface consists of sand between 0.063 and 0.125mm in size. ADatP-3 BL11, BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1423/4 adapted - Def MIP-DMSWG SANDVF 1000018
Smooth and flat The surface is stable and smooth and contains ripples of less than 15cm. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1423/3 adapted - Def MIP-DMSWG SMTHFL 1000019
Stable and smooth The surface is stable and smooth and contains holes, bumps and folds up to 30cm. ADatP-3 BL13.1 FFIRN/FUD 1423/3 adapted - Def MIP-DMSWG STBLSM 1000020
Not otherwise specified The appropriate value is not in the set of specified values. ATCCIS NOS 1000021
