Domain
Name: object-item-hostility-status-code
Definition: The specific value that represents the perceived hostility status of a specific OBJECT-ITEM.
Definition Source: MIP-NDAG
Allowable Values:
Value Definition Source Physical Value Identifier
Assumed friend An OBJECT-ITEM that is assumed to be a friend because of its characteristics, behaviour or origin. Adapted STANAG 1241 AFR 1000001
Assumed hostile An indication that the OBJECT-ITEM in question is likely to belong to enemy forces. ATCCIS AHO 1000002
Assumed involved An indication that the OBJECT-ITEM in question is likely to belong to involved forces different from own, allied and enemy forces. ATCCIS AIV 1000003
Assumed neutral An indication that the OBJECT-ITEM in question is likely to belong to neither own, allied, enemy or otherwise involved forces. ATCCIS ANT 1000004
Faker An OBJECT-ITEM that is a friendly aircraft simulating a hostile aircraft in an air defence exercise. Adapted APP-6A OCT 98 FAKER 1000009
Friend An OBJECT-ITEM that belongs to a declared friendly nation. Adapted STANAG 1241 FR 1000005
Hostile An OBJECT-ITEM that is positively identified as enemy. Adapted STANAG 1241 HO 1000006
Involved An indication that the OBJECT-ITEM in question belongs to involved forces different from own, allied and enemy forces. ATCCIS IV 1000007
Joker An OBJECT-ITEM that is acting as a suspect track for exercise purposes only. Adapted STANAG 1241 JOKER 1000010
Neutral An OBJECT-ITEM whose characteristics, behaviour, origin or nationality indicate that it is neither supporting friendly nor opposing forces. Adapted STANAG 1241 NEUTRL 1000008
Pending An OBJECT-ITEM for which identification is to be determined. Adapted STANAG 1241 PENDNG 1000011
Suspect An OBJECT-ITEM that is potentially hostile because of its characteristics, behaviour or origin. Adapted STANAG 1241 SUSPCT 1000012
Unknown An OBJECT-ITEM for which its hostility status information is not available. Adapted APP-6A OCT 98 UNK 1000013
