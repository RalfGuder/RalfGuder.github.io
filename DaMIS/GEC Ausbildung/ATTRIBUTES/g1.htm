Attribut: geographic-feature-status-code

| Index | 


Definition
The specific value that represents or denotes the status of a specific GEOGRAPHIC-FEATURE.
Attributwerte
Cleared A status indicating that terrain encompassed by a GEOGRAPHIC-FEATURE is passable or traversable.
Denied A status indicating that a GEOGRAPHIC-FEATURE is unavailable through means such as removal, contamination or erection of obstructions.
Destroyed Subjectively judged (by the reporting ORGANISATION) to be rendered useless or ineffective.
Heavily damaged Subjectively judged (by the reporting ORGANISATION) to be heavily damaged.
Lightly damaged Subjectively judged (by the reporting ORGANISATION) to be only lightly damaged.
Moderately damaged Subjectively judged (by the reporting ORGANISATION) to be moderately damaged.
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable.
