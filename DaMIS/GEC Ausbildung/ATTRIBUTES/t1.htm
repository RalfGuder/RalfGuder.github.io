Attribut: target-acquisition-employment-description-code

| Index | 


Definition
The specific value that represents or denotes the detailed technical method an acoustic or electromagnetic contact is detected.
Attributwerte
Acoustic, bottom bounce The actual CONTACT is to be made or will be made by acoustic means, using a bottom bounce sound propagation path.
Convergence zone The actual CONTACT is to be made or will be made by acoustic means, using a convergence zone sound propagation path.
Direct path The actual CONTACT is to be made or will be made by acoustic means, using a direct path zone sound propagation path.
Explosive echo The actual CONTACT is to be made or will be made by acoustic means, using the echo of single or multiple explosion.
Infrared The actual CONTACT is to be made or will be made by infrared frequency means.
Magnetic The actual CONTACT is to be made or will be made by magnetic means.
Not otherwise specified The appropriate value is not in the set of specified values.
Radio The actual CONTACT is to be made or will be made by radio frequency means.
Visual The actual CONTACT is to be made or will be made by visual means.
