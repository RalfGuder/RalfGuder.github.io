Attribut: person-status-duty-status-code

| Index | 


Definition
The specific value that represents or denotes the availability for duty of a specific PERSON.
Attributwerte
Absent Not present at the place of duty for an as yet unspecified reason and has not been posted as either deceased or missing.
Arrested Being held by friendly forces Military Police or Civilian Police Force on suspicion of having committed an offence against either Military or Civil Law.
At duty Present at the place of duty.
Deceased A status indicating that a PERSON (or quantity of PERSON-TYPEs) is dead.
Hospitalised A status indicating that a PERSON (or quantity of PERSON-TYPEs) is in a hospital.
Hostage Has been captured by opposing forces or a terrorist organisation and is being held for the purpose of negotiation.
Missing Is missing but has not been posted as absent or deceased.
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable.
On leave A status indicating that a PERSON (or quantity of PERSON-TYPEs) is legally absent from duty.
Prisoner of war Has been captured by opposing forces and is being held in internment.
Refugee Has, because of real or imagined danger, moved of the person's own volition, spontaneously or in violation of a stay-put policy, irrespective of whether the move is within their own country or across international boundaries.
