Attribut: visibility-direction-code

| Index | 


Definition
The specific value that represents or denotes the direction for which a specific VISIBILITY is valid.
Attributwerte
All directions In any or all directions at one time.
Down In a direction toward the centre of earth or a comparable gravitational centre.
East The cardinal point on a compass located at 90 degrees.
East Northeast A direction between East and Northeast.
East Southeast A direction between East and Southeast.
North The cardinal point on a compass located at 0 degree.
North Northeast A direction between North and Northeast.
North Northwest A direction between North and Northwest.
Northeast The cardinal point on a compass located at 45 degrees.
Northwest The cardinal point on a compass located at 315 degrees.
South The cardinal point on a compass located at 180 degrees.
South Southeast A direction between South and Southeast.
South Southwest A direction between South and Southwest.
Southeast The cardinal point on a compass located at 135 degrees.
Southwest The cardinal point on a compass located at 225 degrees.
Up In a direction opposite to the centre of earth or a comparable gravitational centre.
West The cardinal point on a compass located at 270 degrees.
West Northwest A direction between West and Northwest.
West Southwest A direction between West and Southwest.
