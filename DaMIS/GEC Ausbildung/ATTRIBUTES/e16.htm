Attribut: equipment-type-mobility-code

| Index | 


Definition
The specific value that represents or denotes the mobility of a particular EQUIPMENT-TYPE.
Attributwerte
Air, fixed wing Any of various winged vehicles capable of flight.
Air, not otherwise specified A device other than a fixed wing or rotary wing aircraft capable of flight.
Air, rotary wing A rotorcraft.
Amphibious A device capable of operating both on land and in water.
Land, over snow A device especially designed for moving over snow.
Land, railed A device capable of moving along rails.
Land, towed A device capable of moving by being pulled.
Land, tracked A device capable of moving on caterpillar treads.
Land, wheeled A device capable of moving on wheels.
Land, wheeled cross country A device capable of moving on wheels off road.
Land, wheeled/tracked A device capable of moving on wheels or caterpillar treads.
Man-portable A device capable of being carried by a person.
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable.
Not otherwise specified The appropriate value is not in the set of specified values.
Sea, subsurface A device capable of moving over or under the sea surface.
Sea, surface A device capable of moving over the sea surface.
Verwandte Attribute
  • land-route-mobility-code
  • unit-type-mobility-code
