Attribut: action-task-status-progress-code

| Index | 


Definition
The specific value that represents or denotes the perceived appraisal of the progress of a specific ACTION-TASK.
Attributwerte
Aborted The specified ACTION-TASK has been abandoned subsequent to its initiation.
Cancelled The specified ACTION-TASK was cancelled prior to its initiation.
Complete The specified ACTION-TASK has been carried out and is complete.
In progress The specified ACTION-TASK is in the process of being carried out at the time of the report.
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable.
Not started The specified ACTION-TASK has not yet begun at the time of the report.
Paused The specified ACTION-TASK has been temporarily halted for an unspecified period of time.
