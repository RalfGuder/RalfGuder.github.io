Attribut: equipment-type-fire-guidance-indicator-code

| Index | 


Definition
The specific value that indicates whether a specific EQUIPMENT-TYPE provides fire guidance.
Attributwerte
No The specific EQUIPMENT-TYPE does not provide fire-guidance.
Yes The specific EQUIPMENT-TYPE provides fire-guidance.
