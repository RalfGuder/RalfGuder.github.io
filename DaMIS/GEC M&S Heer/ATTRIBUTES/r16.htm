Attribut: reporting-data-absolute-timing-effective-time-precision-code

| Index | 


Definition
The specific value that represents the uncertainty in the estimate of the effective date and time for a specific REPORTING-DATA-ABSOLUTE-TIMING.
Attributwerte
Day The date and time are defined to the accuracy of a day.
Hour The date and time are defined to the accuracy of an hour.
Minute The date and time are defined to the accuracy of a minute.
Month The date and time are defined to the accuracy of a month.
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable.
Second The date and time are defined to the accuracy of a second.
Week The date and time are defined to the accuracy of a week.
Year The date and time are defined to the accuracy of a year.
