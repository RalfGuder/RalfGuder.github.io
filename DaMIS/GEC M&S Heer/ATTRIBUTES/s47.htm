Attribut: support-unit-type-arm-code

| Index | 


Definition
The specific value that represents or denotes the arm designation of a particular SUPPORT-UNIT-TYPE.
Attributwerte
Administrative A unit performing those functions that are primarily of a management or administrative nature.
Bridge laying Providing tactical bridging from tracked or armoured vehicles.
Combat service support Tasked to provide support to combat forces, primarily in the fields of administration and logistics.
Construction Building and repairing structures, roads, runways, and infrastructure.
Electronic warfare Tasked with the use of electromagnetic energy to determine, exploit, reduce or prevent hostile use of the electromagnetic spectrum, and action to retain its effective use by friendly forces.
Engineer, armoured A unit that accomplishes major construction, demolition, and extensive camouflage projects, and is specifically equipped with armoured vehicles.
Engineer, not otherwise specified A unit that accomplishes major construction, demolition, and extensive camouflage projects.
Explosive ordnance disposal Tasked with rendering explosive ordnance devices safe.
Headquarters support Tasked with the provision and control of resources to assist and support the staff of a headquarters.
Intelligence Tasked with the processing of information concerning foreign nations, hostile or potentially hostile forces or elements, or areas of actual or potential operations.
Labour resources Tasked with the provision, acquisition or control of labour resources.
Maintenance Tasked with the repair and maintenance of equipment.
Medical Tasked with the provision of medical and dental services and the evacuation of casualties.
Medical, armoured Tasked with the provision of medical and dental services and the evacuation of casualties. The unit uses armoured vehicles.
Meteorological Tasked with the provision of meteorological information.
Military civilian affairs Tasked with the handling of military-civilian relations.
Military police Tasked with route control and prevention and detection of crimes.
Minefield Providing mine-laying capability.
Multirole A UNIT-TYPE combining two or more of the functions maintenance, quartermaster/logistics, and transportation/movement.
NBC Tasked with undertaking or assisting protective measures against NBC effects.
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable.
Not otherwise specified The appropriate value is not in the set of specified values.
Ordnance A unit responsible for the supply and maintenance of weapons, weapon system components, ammunition, and related equipment.
Pay/finance Tasked with the provision of pay, accounting and financial services.
Personnel services Tasked with the provision of services in support of personnel and their administration.
Pipeline Tasked with the provision, maintenance and management of pipelines for the passage of bulk liquids.
Postal and courier Tasked with the provision of postal and courier services.
Psychological Tasked with the conduct of psychological operations.
Quartermaster/logistics Tasked with the provision of support to the soldier, UNITs, and groups of ORGANISATIONs, including the distribution of combat supplies and general materiel.
Ranging, electronic Tasked with the location of artillery and other indirect fire weapons by the use of electronic equipment.
Reinforcement/replacement holding unit Tasked with the management and delivery of reinforcement and/or replacement of personnel or some types of equipment (such as main battle tanks).
Road/railway Providing support to the construction of railways and cross-loading stores by means of rail-handling equipment.
Signals Tasked with the provision of communications bearer systems.
Sound ranging Tasked with the location of artillery and other indirect fire equipment by the use of sound ranging equipment.
Supply Tasked to provide all material and items used in the equipping, supporting and maintaining of military forces.
Survey Tasked with the provision of and control of surveying, mapping and associated services.
Transportation, medical Tasked with the provision of specialised transport for wounded and/or ill personnel.
Transportation/movement Tasked with the provision of road transport for personnel and/or materiel.
Veterinary Tasked with the provision of veterinary services.
