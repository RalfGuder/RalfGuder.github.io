Attribut: candidate-target-list-authorisation-indicator-code

| Index | 


Definition
The specific value that denotes whether a specific CANDIDATE-TARGET-LIST is authorised further consideration in planning military operations.
Attributwerte
No The specific CANDIDATE-TARGET-LIST is not authorised further consideration in planning military operations.
Yes The subject CANDIDATE-TARGET-LIST is authorised further consideration in planning military operations.
