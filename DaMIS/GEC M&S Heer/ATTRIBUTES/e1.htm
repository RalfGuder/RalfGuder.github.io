Attribut: electromagnetic-feature-electromagnetic-feature-association-description-code

| Index | 


Definition
The specific value that represents or denotes the type of relationship between the subject ELECTROMAGNETIC-FEATURE and the object ELECTROMAGNETIC-FEATURE in a specific ELECTROMAGNETIC-FEATURE-ELECTROMAGNETIC-FEATURE-ASSOCIATION.
Attributwerte
Contains A relationship between two ELECTROMAGNETIC-FEATUREs which denotes that the whole of the subject ELECTROMAGNETIC-FEATURE encompasses the object ELECTROMAGNETIC-FEATURE.
Is successor of A relationship between ELECTROMAGNETIC-FEATUREs which denotes an ordering in which the subject ELECTROMAGNETIC-FEATURE is the successor of the object ELECTROMAGNETIC-FEATURE.
