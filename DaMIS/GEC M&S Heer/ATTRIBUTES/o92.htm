Attribut: organisation-type-qualifier-code

| Index | 


Definition
The specific value that describes whether a specific ORGANISATION-TYPE is a civil, military, governmental or other one.
Attributwerte
Civil An indication that the specified ORGANISATION-TYPE is a civil one.
Governmental An indication that the specified ORGANISATION-TYPE is a governmental one.
Military An indication that the specified ORGANISATION-TYPE is a military one.
