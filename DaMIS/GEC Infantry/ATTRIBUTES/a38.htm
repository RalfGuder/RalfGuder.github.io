Attribut: action-objective-qualifier-code

| Index | 


Definition
The specific value that represents or denotes a restriction or other qualification applicable to a specific ACTION-OBJECTIVE for a specific ACTION.
Attributwerte
Authorised The ACTION-OBJECTIVE is authorised without restriction.
No exploitation east of line The specific ACTION-OBJECTIVE is authorised with the restriction that there is to be no exploitation east of a specified line (be it a CONTROL-FEATURE, northing, line of longitude, etc.).
No exploitation north of line The specific ACTION-OBJECTIVE is authorised with the restriction that there is to be no exploitation north of a specified line (be it a CONTROL-FEATURE, easting, line of latitude etc.).
No exploitation south of line The specific ACTION-OBJECTIVE is authorised with the restriction that there is to be no exploitation south of a specified line (be it a CONTROL-FEATURE, easting, line of latitude, etc.).
No exploitation west of line The specific ACTION-OBJECTIVE is authorised with the restriction that there is to be no exploitation west of a specified line (be it a CONTROL-FEATURE, northing, line of longitude, etc.).
Not authorised The specific ACTION-OBJECTIVE is not authorised.
Stay above area The specific ACTION-OBJECTIVE is authorised with the restriction that there is to be no movement below a given Air Operations Zone.
Stay below area The specific ACTION-OBJECTIVE is authorised with the restriction that there is to be no movement within a given Air Operations Zone/above a given altitude.
Stay inside area The specific ACTION-OBJECTIVE is authorised with the restriction that there is to be no movement outside a given area.
Stay outside area The specific ACTION-OBJECTIVE is authorised with the restriction that there is to be no operations within a given area.
