Attribut: organisation-person-association-description-code

| Index | 


Definition
The specific value that represents or denotes the type of relationship between the subject ORGANISATION and the object PERSON for a specific ORGANISATION-PERSON-ASSOCIATION.
Attributwerte
Has as a liaison officer Placed a person with an ORGANISATION on a temporary basis with the objective of coordinating actions.
Has on assignment Placed a person with the ORGANISATION, permanently.
Has on attachment Placed a person with the ORGANISATION, temporarily.
Is captor of Has captured a PERSON.
Is under command of The ORGANISATION has as its authorised leader a specific PERSON.
