Attribut: unit-type-size-code

| Index | 


Definition
The specific value that represents or denotes the lowest structural level or point at which organisational control or authority of a specific UNIT-TYPE is concentrated.
Attributwerte
Air-crew All personnel operating an aircraft.
Air-wing An air wing is the basic organizational and administrative unit for naval-, land-, and tender-based aviation. Such wings are mobile units to which are assigned aircraft squadrons and tenders for administrative organization control.
Army A formation whose map symbol is 'XXXX'.
Army group A formation whose map symbol is 'XXXXX'.
Battalion A unit whose map symbol is 'II'.
Battalion group A unit whose map symbol is 'II' with a +-+ over it.
Brigade A formation whose map symbol is 'X'.
Company A unit whose map symbol is 'I' (The value 'Company' includes squadron and battery).
Company group A unit whose map symbol is 'I' with a +-+ over it.
Corps A formation whose map symbol is 'XXX'.
Division A formation whose map symbol is 'XX'.
Flight A unit whose map symbol is 'I' (A military air unit).
Flottilla A navy organisational unit consisting of two or more squadrons.
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable.
Platoon A unit whose map symbol is '???'.
Regiment A unit whose map symbol is 'III'.
Section A unit whose map symbol is '??'.
Squad A unit whose map symbol is '?'.
Squadron An organization consisting of two or more divisions of ships, or two or more divisions (Navy) or flights of aircraft. It is normally, but not necessarily, composed of ships or aircraft of the same type.
Team/fire team/crew Any unit smaller than a squad which will be denoted by a vehicle or weapon symbol in a graphical representation.
