Attribut: frequency-qualifier-code

| Index | 


Definition
A specific code which describes a certain frequency or frequency range as a fundamental or harmonic frequency, which states a multiple of a fundamental frequency.
Attributwerte
Centred-frequency-range A frequency range which is centred around a specific frequency value.
Frequency-range A range of frequencies
Fundamental-frequency A basic or ground frequency.
Harmonic-frequency A whole multiple of a fundamental frequency.
Not otherwise specified A frequency which is neither a fundamental nor a harmonic frequency.
