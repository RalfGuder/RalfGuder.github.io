Attribut: unit-type-service-code

| Index | 


Definition
The specific value that represents or denotes a military, paramilitary, or irregular force or group capable of functioning as an offensive or defensive combat or support organisation.
Attributwerte
Air Force The UNIT-TYPE in question belongs to the Air Force (includes reserves and mobilised air national guard).
Army The UNIT-TYPE in question belongs to the Army (includes territorial army, reserves, and mobilised national guard).
Border guard A paramilitary UNIT-TYPE whose primary task is to maintain the security of national borders.
Coast guard The organisation that may be responsible for one or more of the following: coastal defense, protection of life and property at sea, and enforcement of customs, immigration, and navigation laws.
Combined The UNIT-TYPE contains two or more forces or agencies of two or more allies.
Guerrilla The UNIT-TYPE in question is an irregular military force.
Internal security force The UNIT-TYPE comprises uniformed, trained personnel. Its primary purpose is the support of civil law within a country.
Joint The UNIT-TYPE contains elements of more than one Service from the same nation.
Local defence force A military UNIT-TYPE whose primary task is defence of a specific region (before mobilisation, a national guard is a local defence force).
Local militia A civilian UNIT-TYPE that has a defence and possibly also a police role (may include irregular civilian UNIT-TYPEs).
Marines The UNIT-TYPE in question belongs to the Marines (includes reserves).
Navy The UNIT-TYPE in question belongs to the Navy (includes reserves).
Not known It is not possible to determine which value is most applicable.
Not otherwise specified The appropriate value is not in the set of specified values.
Special force The UNIT-TYPE in question is trained and equipped for special purposes.
Territorial force The branch of a nation's armed forces that is responsible for regional defence.
